After efforts to repeal and replace Barack Obama's Affordable Care Act collapsed, US President Donald Trump has said that Republicans should "let Obamacare fail". 
"I think we're probably in that position where we'll let Obamacare fail. 
We're not going to own it. 
I'm not going to own it," Trump added. 
Notably, around 2 crore people gained health coverage under Obamacare.
