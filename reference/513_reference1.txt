A doctor named Praveen Yadav, who was accused in Madhya Pradesh's Vyapam scam, allegedly committed suicide at his residence on Wednesday. 
Yadav had cleared his Pre-Medical Test (PMT) in 2012 and was running an oil mill. 
As per reports, more than 40 people associated with the scam have died since reports related to the scam came to light in 2013.
