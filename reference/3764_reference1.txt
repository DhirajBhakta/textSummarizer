Former Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) MP Mohammad Shahabuddin has been acquitted by a Jamshedpur court in a triple murder case that took place on February 2, 1989. 
Notably, three out of the eight accused died during the trial. 
Shahabuddin, who is linked to 45 criminal cases, is currently lodged in Delhi's high-security Tihar Jail.
