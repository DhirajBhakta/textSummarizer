The Rashtrapati Bhavan, the residence of India's President, took 17 years to finish building, and was earlier the Viceroy's House. 
The complex has a total of 340 rooms, spread over four floors, and an area of 2,00,000 square feet. 
A part of the complex are the Mughal Gardens, one of the world's best rose gardens with over 150 varieties.
