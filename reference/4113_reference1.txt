A 22-year-old man, Rahat Khan, has been arrested by the police in Greater Noida for allegedly posting a morphed image of newly elected Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath on his Facebook wall. A complaint was lodged against Khan, who captioned the image, 'Yogi hai, ya bhogi hai', by members of Hindu Yuva Vahini which was founded by Adityanath.
