Celebrity chef Vikas Khanna gifted a copy of his cookbook 'Utsav: A Culinary Epic of Indian Festivals' to Queen Elizabeth II at the launch of the UK-India Year of Culture 2017 at Buckingham Palace on Monday. 
The 1,200-page limited-edition cookbook covers over 70 festivals across India and includes 1,000 recipes.
