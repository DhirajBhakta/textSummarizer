The Vishwa Hindu Parishad (VHP) has decided to recruit 5,000 'religious soldiers' in Uttar Pradesh's Aligarh district, to control cow smuggling and love jihad in the region. 
Only Bajrang Dal activists who have completed bravery training will be eligible for the post, recruitment for which will start in September. 
They will also protect "Hindu boys and girls, maths and temples".
