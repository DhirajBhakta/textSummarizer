Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal on Monday approved the proposal to construct 6,178 flats to rehabilitate the slum dwellers living in Lajpat Nagar, Bhalswa, Dev Nagar, Mangol Puri and Ambedkar Nagar. "The 6178 EWS flats will be constructed at the cost of 866 crore," the Delhi government stated. AAP governments biggest poll policy was 'Jaha Jhuggi, Wahan Makaan', it added.
