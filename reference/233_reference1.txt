Agra has had 53% lower rainfall this year, with just 123.5 mm rainfall, according to the Meteorological Department. 
Five districts across UP? 
Agra, Pilibhit, Muzaffarnagar, Ghaziabad and Noida had seriously low rainfall and are facing drought conditions, reports said. 
Noida received only 96 mm rains against the average of 204.2 mm, making a shortfall of 53%.
