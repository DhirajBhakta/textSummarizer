A special CBI court on Tuesday charged Sheena Bora murder case's key accused - Indrani Mukerjea, Peter Mukerjea and Sanjeev Khanna - with murder and criminal conspiracy. 
The CBI had stated that Indrani had killed Sheena with the assistance of her ex-husband Sanjeev and driver Shyamvar. 
The CBI had also said that Indrani's husband Peter was aware of the crime.
