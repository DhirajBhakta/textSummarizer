The passenger who allegedly opened the emergency exit of a Chandigarh-bound IndiGo flight before takeoff was released on a surety of 15,000 on Saturday. Airport Police said 31-year-old Akshay Rameshkumar Mohan was released as this was a bailable offence. IndiGo had earlier claimed that a passenger suddenly opened the emergency exit and inflated the slide, causing injury to a co-passenger.
