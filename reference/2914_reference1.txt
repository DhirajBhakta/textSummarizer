Delhi's crime rate has fallen by 31.54% this year, with the police managing to solve 65.42% cases reported this year. 
This is a 16% increase in the solve rate, which stood at 45.15% in 2015. 
Till May 15 last year, 802 rape cases were reported, while the figure stands at 740 this year, with 77.3% cases reported to be solved.
