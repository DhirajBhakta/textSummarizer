The Delhi Metro on Friday announced that it will make 10 of its stations cashless from January 1 by opting for digital payment app Paytm for token purchase and smart card recharge. 
The stations chosen are Rohini East, Rohini West, MG Road Station, Mayur Vihar Phase-I, Nirman Vihar, Tilak Nagar, Janakpuri West, Noida Sector-15, Nehru Place, and Kailash Colony.
