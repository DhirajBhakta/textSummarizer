Cyclone Vardah made its landfall near Chennai, Tamil Nadu, on Monday and all flight operations at the city airport have been suspended until 9PM. 
According to officials, over 4,600 people living in low-lying areas have been shifted to relief camps in Chennai, Kanchipuram, Thiruvallur, and Villupuram. 
The National Disaster Response Force and the armed forces have been put on alert.
