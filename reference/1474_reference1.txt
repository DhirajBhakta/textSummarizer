Terming Tamil Nadu government's ordinance to allow Jallikattu as a temporary solution, residents of Alanganallur in Madurai have vowed to not allow the conduct of the festival. 
Tamil Nadu CM O Panneerselvam was scheduled to inaugurate the bull-taming festival from Alanganallur on Sunday. 
Panneerselvam had earlier promised to pass a new law in the upcoming Assembly session allowing Jallikattu.
