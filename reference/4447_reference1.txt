Radhanagar beach on Havelock Island in the Andamans has been ranked the world's 8th best in TripAdvisor's 'World's Travellers Choice Award-Winning Beaches'. The beach has also secured the first spot in Asia. The Baia do Sancho beach in Brazil has topped the list, followed by Grace Bay in the Turks and Caicos Islands and Eagle Beach in Aruba.
