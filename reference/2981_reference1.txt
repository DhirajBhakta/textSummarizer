Mahmood Shah, the chief of terrorist organisation Lashkar-e-Taiba (LeT), in an official statement, said the "sacrifice" of LeT commander Junaid Mattoo and other militants, killed in an encounter with Indian armed forces, will not go in vain. 
Meanwhile, LeT spokesperson Dr. 
Abdullah Ghaznavi called India "a cancer to humanity", while adding, "We shall soon witness India burning to ashes."
