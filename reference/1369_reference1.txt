Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Monday paid tribute to Mahatma Gandhi, the Father of the Nation, on his 69th death anniversary. 
Modi took to Twitter and said, "Tributes to beloved Bapu on his 'Punya Tithi' (death anniversary)." 
Notably, Mahatma Gandhi was assassinated by Nathuram Godse on January 30, 1948, during a prayer meeting at Delhi's Birla House.
