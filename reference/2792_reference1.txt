Evita Delmundo, who was born with large moles all over her face and body, has auditioned for the Miss Universe Malaysia beauty pageant. 
"I learnt to accept my birthmarks and love myself. 
I slowly gained confidence to show off my uniqueness," said Evita. 
The pageant's official Facebook page described Evita as an "empowering 20-year-old" while sharing an article on her.
