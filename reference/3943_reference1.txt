An art exhibition named 'Cadmium Skies' in Delhi will showcase the works of 26 children that have been inspired by painter Vincent Van Gogh. 
Recreations of Van Gogh's famous works such as 'Starry Night' and 'Sunflowers' will be on display. 
Original creations where the participants have employed Van Gogh's techniques and not recreated his work will also be displayed.
