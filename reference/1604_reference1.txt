Pakistan on Thursday handed over Javed Iqbal, an Indian national who had inadvertently crossed the LoC on May 3, 2016, to the Indian Army. 
Javed was handed over at Chakan Da Bagh crossing point along LoC in Poonch district of Jammu and Kashmir. 
Iqbal's return was made possible after continuous pursuance by the Indian Army, said a defence official.
