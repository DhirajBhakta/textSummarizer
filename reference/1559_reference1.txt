Petr Nitka, a model from the Czech Republic, will become the first gender neutral model to walk at India's Lakm? Fashion Week in its upcoming Summer/Resort 2017 edition. 
Petr has walked for both menswear and womenswear. 
"With every season, Lakm? Fashion Week is breaking beauty boundaries and showcasing diversity," said Purnima Lamba, Head of Innovation at Lakm?.
