FasoPro, a startup based in African nation Burkina Faso creates products like salted cakes and pepper chips with protein-rich caterpillar powder to tackle malnutrition. 
The caterpillars, which feed on the leaves of shea trees, are made up of more than 60% protein. 
The startup was founded by Kahitouo Hien, a graduate of the University of California, Berkeley in 2011.
