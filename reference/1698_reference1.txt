Fourteen-year-old Darpan Basak and thirteen-year-old Ankit Kumar have won the Travel Photographer of the Year award 2016. 
While Darpan was named the 'Young Photographer of the Year', Ankit won the award in the 14 years old and under age-group in the 'Places and Experiences' category. 
The award was founded by photographers Chris and Karen Coe in 2003.n
