Rana Daggubati and Prabhas play arch-enemies Bhallala Deva and Baahubali on screen. 
While Prabhas is Baahubali, a man who is kind, benevolent, noble and brave, Rana Daggubati is Bhallala Deva, a conniving, bitter, arrogant and evil person. 
But in real life, these two are the best of friends.Prabhas and Rana Daggubati shot for Baahubali: The Beginning and Baahubali: The Conclusion for up to five years. 
And as it goes with close friends, these two played pranks on each other.Rana Daggubati in a recent episode of the chat show Konchem Touch lo Unte Chepta spilled out the beans about one such prank.
