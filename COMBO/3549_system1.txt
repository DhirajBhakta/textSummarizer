Yogi Adityanath has set a deadline for filling up all the potholes in Uttar Pradesh by June 15.Yogi Adityanath said, "We have asked (the concerned authorities) to ensure by June 15 Uttar Pradesh should become pothole-free."
Taking a jibe on the previous Akhilesh Yadav government, Yogi Adityanath said, "It had become the identity of Uttar Pradesh that the spot from where potholes appear and where darkness sets in at the stroke of evening, the boundary of UP starts."
Yogi Adityanath's comment is interesting in the wake of his predecessor Akhilesh Yadav's public claim about improving the roads of Uttar Pradesh.
