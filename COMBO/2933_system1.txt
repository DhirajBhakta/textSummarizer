Time has come for India to take decisions with focus on the 2019 World Cup and that includes a call on the role of MS Dhoni and Yuvraj Singh in the current team, feels former captain Rahul Dravid."
It is a call that is got to be taken by the selectors and the management," Dravid told 'ESPNCricinfo' when asked about future of Yuvraj and Dhoni, who were the team's number four and five in the Champions Trophy."
Do you want to look at the available talent and see what they have to offer before going back to these two players," he elaborated further.India will play the limited overs series in the West Indies beginning Friday with a full strength squad.
