On its fifth week, Baahubali 2: The Conclusion is running to packed houses and has grossed Rs 1600 crore worldwide. 
Ever since its release. the Prabhas-starrer has been smashing records with its box-office collection.Speaking about the blockbuster success of Baahubali 2, Karan said, "I feel short of words when I speak about Baahubali. 
Karan Johar distributed Baahubali 2: The Conclusion and its predecessor in Hindi."
I am honoured that me and my production house got associated with Baahubali and I am proud and privileged to be a part of its journey," Karan added.Directed by SS Rajamouli, the film stars Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Ramya Krishnan and Sathyaraj in pivotal roles.
