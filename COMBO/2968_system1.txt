New Delhi, Jun 17 (PTI) Two men have been arrested and a juvenile apprehended for allegedly killing an 18-year-old boy after he refused to lend his scooter to one of the accused in northeast Delhis Nand Nagri area, police said today. 
On June 15, Gulfam was stabbed and beaten by the three accused in a park after he was forcibly taken there from his house, the police said. 
His sister told police that Karan, Sunny and the juvenile took her brother to a park. 
Karan and Sunny caught hold of Gulfam while the juvenile attacked him with a knife, said the police officer.
