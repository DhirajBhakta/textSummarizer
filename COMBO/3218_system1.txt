Speculations were rife about what Saif Ali Khan's daughter Sara Ali Khan's debut project would be.At various times, she was attached to Karan Johar's Student of the Year 2 and at one time with a film opposite Hrithik Roshan. 
The list also included a possible Abhishek Kapoor project starring Sushant Singh Rajput.Now, Abhishek Kapoor himself has confirmed exclusively to India Today that Sara Ali Khan will indeed be making her Bollywood debut opposite Sushant Singh Rajput in a film called Kedarnath.Earlier, in an interview to Mumbai Mirror, Saif Ali Khan had said that even though she understands his daughter Sara's willingness to join the profession, he is also worried."
