 The state government has planned to implement cycling tracks along the arterial roads in 10 leading cities of the state. 
The committee will identify arterial roads where cycle tracks can be set up depending on the availability of space and the scope for cycling. 
It will also have to design the routes for these tracks and plan any other requirements or alterations to the traffic flow on the roads hosting the cycle tracks, an official from the state public works department said.Read: Is Mumbai ready for cycle tracks? 
Some of the previous efforts at creating cycling tracks and promoting bicycles have failed miserably owing to lack of proper planning and maintenance.
