A young man was forced to carry his unconscious, and injured mother on foot? until help came by. 
He requested several people for help and even requested for an ambulance but did not get it in time.The incident took place at Udanoor cross in Kalburgi where Siddamma was walking with her son Ninganna. 
She had come to the city to meet her daughter. 
Siddama was rammed by a motorbike. 
Immediately after the accident, locals rushed to the spot and Ninganna found her mother lying in a pool of blood. 
She suffered head and leg injuries. 
Ninganna pleaded with several people around to help him take his mother to a hospital.
