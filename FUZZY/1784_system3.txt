Justice RM Lodha today hailed the landmark order delivered by the Supreme Court which axed BCCI president Anurag Thakur and secretary Ajay Shirke from their posts. 
(How BCCI lost control of cricket in India: A timeline)The two top BCCI officials were defiant and did not implement the Lodha reforms despite a clear order by the apex court. 
Lodha, speaking to India Today, said he was happy and reiterated that there could have been no middle path in the implementation of the reforms after the Supreme Court had delivered an order. 
Meanwhile, the Supreme Court made it clear that defiant heads of state associations who did not agree to implement the Lodha reforms would also be removed.
