Panaji, Jul 24 (PTI) Goa Chief Minister Manohar Parrikar today assured the House that no more permissions for casinos -? 
In yet another announcement, Parrikar also said that the number of off shore casinos in Mandovi river would be capped to existing five vessels, which?would be shifted to land in next three years. 
Parrikar said that in the existing act, the state government has power to cap the number of casinos in a particular area.
