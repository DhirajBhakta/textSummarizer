The WVMs are operated round-the-clock either manually or via automatic mode.The water dispensed through these machines will be much cheaper than bottled mineral water, the ministry said.While a glass of half a litre water will cost Rs 3, a litre will be available Rs 5, two litres for Rs 8 and a can for Rs 20.
