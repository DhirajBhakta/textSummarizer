Pakistan has placed Jamaat-ud- Dawa chief Hafiz Saeed's name on the Exit Control List on Wednesday, two days after the Mumbai attack mastermind and his four aides were put under house arrest.The Interior Ministry has forwarded a letter to all provincial governments and the Federal Investigation Agency, which included names of 38 individuals placed on the list, preventing them from leaving the country.
