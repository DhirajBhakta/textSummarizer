Mumbai, May 1 (PTI) Ace shuttler Pusarla Venkata Sindhu, who became the first Indian woman to win a silver medal at the Olympics last year, is the next sports personality whose story is heading to the big screen. 
Actor-producer Sonu Sood is set to paint Sindhus life and victory on the silver screen. 
"It feels great to make a biopic on PV Sindhu . 
Its a story of a girl who have inspired millions of Indians and given a message that everyone can dream big and not only dream but achieve it too by doing hard work. 
"Its a journey that everyone has to know and be inspired," Sonu said in a statement here.
