Former Australian captain Ian Chappell feels India have run out of ideas to dismiss Steve Smith in the ongoing Test series against the men from down under. 
Smith is the highest scorer in the four-match series. 
(Ranchi Test: Australia hold on for draw, series-decider in Dharamsala)Chappell felt it was apparent India's bowlers "are no closer to unlocking the secret to upending Smith." 
"Smith was already a successful batting captain, but on this tour of India he has taken his play to another level," Chappell wrote in his column for ESPNcricinfo. 
"He's made two centuries in three Tests, and following his undefeated vigil in Ranchi, it's apparent India's bowlers are no closer to unlocking the secret to upending him.
