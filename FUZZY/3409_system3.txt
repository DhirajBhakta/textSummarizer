Gujarat Deputy Chief Minister Nitin Patel's son was early on Monday prevented by authorities from boarding a Qatar Airways flight to Greece after he arrived in a "heavily drunken" state at the airport and argued with the airline's staff.Official sources told IANS that Jaimin Patel, who is in his mid-30s, his wife Jhalak and their daughter Vaishvi were stopped from boarding the Qatar Airways flight, which was to take off at 4 a.m.When Patel, who is a land dealer, reached Ahmedabad International Airport, he was not even able to walk because of his drunken state. 
He cleared the immigration and other checks in a wheelchair due to his inebriated condition, said airport officials."
