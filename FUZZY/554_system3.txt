The director and chief executive officer (healthcare) of Amrapali Group were arrested by tehsildar of Dadri on Monday afternoon from their corporate office in Sector 62 for not paying labour cess amounting to Rs 4.29 crore to the labour department. 
Another director, Mohit Gupta, was also to be arrested but he was not in his office at the time of the arrests.Section 171 of Uttar Pradesh Revenue Code says any person defaulting on payment of an arrear of land revenue may be arrested and detained in custody in the tehsil lockup till the dues are paid but the period should not exceed 15 days. 
The district magistrate said the revenue department received a recovery certificate from the labour department on June 5 for recovery of Rs 4.29 crore from the Amrapali Group.
