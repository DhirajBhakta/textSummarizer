A day ahead of the MCD election 2017, Swaraj India leader Yogendra Yadav has accused Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal of betraying the trust of the people of the city.Yogendra Yadav also sought Arvind Kejriwal's resignation "if the AAP fails to win 136 (50 per cent) seats" in the MCD elections.Writing a letter to his former India Against Corruption colleague, Yogendra Yadav said, "You have broken the trust of the people of Delhi," before adding, "Following your betrayal, the public has lost faith in its own capability to distinguish good from bad."
