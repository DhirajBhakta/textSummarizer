Director Kabir Khan lashed out at the Central Board of Film Certification and its chairperson Pahlaj Nihalani over the CBFC's denial of a certificate for Alankrita Srivastava's film, Lipstick Under My Burkha. 
The film was also widely celebrated at the Tokyo Film Festival and MAMI (Mumbai Film Festival) last year.Speaking exclusively to India Today, Kabir Khan said that his first reaction to the news was, "Oh god, not again! 
The man behind blockbusters like Ek Tha Tiger and Bajrangi Bhaijaan said that every two months, the CBFC had made it a habit to do something completely stupid like this.Kabir, whose next Bollywood outing will be Salman Khan-starrer Tubelight, added that the time had come for the film industry to unite and protest for their rights.
