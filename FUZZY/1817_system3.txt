New Delhi, Dec 29 (PTI) RBIs newly appointed Deputy Governor Viral Acharya, a noted economist, today said he will give his best in the new role. 
"I am honoured at being offered the opportunity and hope to give it my best," Acharya told PTI a day after being named the Deputy Governor. 
He would look after the Monetary Policy and Research cluster and would fill the post that became vacant after Urjit Patel was made RBI Governor. 
Currently, Acharya is serving as C V Starr Professor of Economics, Department of Finance at the New York University- Stern School of Business. 
Like former RBI Governor Raghuram Rajan, Acharya comes from an academic background and has also co-authored at least three papers with him.
