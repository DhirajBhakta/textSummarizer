A BJP MLA from Uttar Pradesh has put up a Facebook video openly saying that he will stop Haj yatris to go on the pilgrimage if the Ram temple is not built in Ayodhya.BJP MLA Brijbhushan Rajput said, "If our Ram temple is not built then why should they be allowed to go to Haj Yatra? 
They should also be stopped from going to Haj Yatra. 
The subsidy should stop"."
