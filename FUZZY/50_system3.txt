 A session court in Mumbai convicted 15 Somali pirates for 7 years of imprisonment in a 2011 case. 
They have been found guilty of attempt to murder and kidnapping. 
This is one of the four cases registered in 2011 against a total of 120 Somali pirates, for holding 91 people from different countries hostage.
