Aamir Khan-starrer PK has become the first film to touch the Rs 300-crore mark in its box office earning in domestic markets.According to Trade Analyst Taran Adarsh, the social satire which invited ire from several religious groups became the first Bollywood film to enter the Rs 300-cr club.Interestingly, the top three films to have first scored the 100, 200 and 300 crore milestone are all Aamir Khan starrers. 
#Ghajini first Hindi film to cross Rs 100 cr. 3Idiots first Hindi film to cross Rs 200 cr. 
#PK first Hindi film to cross Rs 300 cr," Adarsh tweeted.
