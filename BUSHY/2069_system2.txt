A constable was attacked with chilli powder and bricks when he tried to stop seven suspicious looking men in south west Delhi?
Police officials said the incident took place around 3.45 am when constable Pawan and home guard Laxmi Narayan were on night duty in the area of Manas Kunj T point at Mangal Bazar road. 
The presence of so many men at such an odd hour alerted Pawan and he stopped them.In his complaint to the police, Pawan said that he became suspicious about them while enquiring them. 
Sensing that the men could attack them, Pawan took out his pistol, made a phone call to Bindapur police station and asked for back-up.
