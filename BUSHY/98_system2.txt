Motorman Mahendra Prasad called up the railway police control room on Sunday and informed officials that a 15-year-old boy and 25-year-old youth had fallen from a train and were found injured on the tracks between Lower Parel and Elphinston stations. 
According to the railway data, 348 people died on the Central line while 323 persons died after falling off trains on the Western line this year till June. 
The GRP officers said that overcrowding is the main reason of people falling off trains which is a concern for the authorities.
