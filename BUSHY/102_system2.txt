New Delhi, Aug 1 (PTI) Flight Lt Shikha Pandey, a member of the Indian womens cricket team, was today felicitated by Indian Air Force chief BS Dhanoa for her sterling performance at the recent World Cup. 
Pandey was awarded the chief of the air staff commendation by Air Chief Marshal Dhanoa for her performance at the recently concluded ICC Women World Cup, the IAF said. 
The Indian womens cricket team finished runner-up, losing to England narrowly by nine runs. 
However, the teams magnificent performance to reach the finals won laurels from all quarters.
