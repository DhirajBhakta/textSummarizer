Ahead of Monsoon Session today, Prime Minister Narendra Modi said that the successful launch of the Goods and Services Tax would infuse fresh energy in the Parliament session.Giving a new definition of the Goods and Services Tax, PM Modi said, "GST means Going Stronger Together". 
Modi also said, "Just as rain brings typical fragrance from the earth, the successful launch of the GST would give new enthusiasm to the Monsoon Session."
The Goods and Services Tax (GST) was launched on July 1 at the special midnight session of Parliament.
