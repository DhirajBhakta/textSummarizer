In the firefight two Army jawans have been martyred.The Border Action Team (BAT), which generally comprises special forces of the Pakistan Army and some terrorists, carried out the attack at around 2 pm with the aid of cover fire by the Pakistani troops from their posts.This was the third BAT action in Poonch this year. 
In a similar BAT attack on May 1, two Indian soldiers were beheaded in Krishna Ghati sector in Poonch district. 
That attack too was carried out under the cover of shelling by the Pakistani troops.Prior to that, a BAT attack was carried out on February 18 but there were no casualties in that.Earlier, there have been several BAT attacks in which Indian jawans have been beheaded or their bodies mutilated.On October 28,last year, militants attacked a post and killed an Indian Army soldier and mutilated his body close to the Line of Control (LoC) in the Machil sector.In January 2013, Lance Naik Hemraj was killed and his body mutilated by a BAT.
