The Right to Information (RTI) application was filed by one Sayak Sen in April wherein he asked if there was any information available with the government on Gumnamibaba or Bhagwanji who lived incognito in Uttar Pradesh till 1985 and was rumoured to be Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose. 
The MHA said that it has declassified all the available (37) files relating to Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose.The RTI application asked if the government has any information regarding the whereabouts of Netaji post August 18, 1945.
