An arranged marriage was supposed to take place between a man and a 20-year-old girl from Rajapalayam on June 1 at Bala Murugan Temple at Ilayampatti.On the wedding day, when the priest asked the man to tie a thali (holy thread) around the bride's neck, the man's younger brother who was standing near him suddenly pulled out another thali kept in his shirt pocked and tied it to the bride. 
Bill in LS seeks cap on guestsThe man's younger brother was standing near him and pushed him aside to tie the thali to the bride, as reported by New Indian Express. 
The younger brother had gone to her house to arrange the marriage for his elder brother. 
Even the girl refused to marry one else despite her family refusing to accept the marriage and forcibly removing her thali.
