Tej Bahadur Yadav, the BSF jawan who was dismissed from duty for uploading a video on the 'substandard food' served to soldiers, today attended a Mumbai event honouring martyrs. 
Apart from condemning the poor quality of food being served, Bahadur had also alleged how officers were siphoning off money sent for the soldiers' upkeep at border areas.He further said, "Government sent us 18 different food items but several times, the authorities gave us only Khichdi... 
Tej Bahadur Yadav along with several other ex-servicemen will stage a one-day protest at Jantar Mantar on May 14. 
The sit-in protest will serve as an ultimatum to government to act on the deeply seated corruption in the armed forces.
