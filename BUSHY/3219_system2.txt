Chanda Kochhar, the head of country's largest private sector lender ICICI Bank, has drawn Rs 7.85 crore as total remuneration in 2016-17, a jump of over nearly 64 per cent from a year ago. 
ICICI Bank MD & CEO Chanda Kochhar's basic salary increased over 15 per cent to Rs 2.67 crore during the fiscal ended March 2017, as per banks annual report. 
During the year, she took home performance bonus of Rs 2.2 crore. 
Her total cost to the company was over Rs 4.79 crore in 2015-16 and basic salary was Rs 2.32 crore. 
The monthly basic salary for Kochhar will be within the range of Rs 1,350,000-Rs 2,600,000, said the annual report 2016-17.
