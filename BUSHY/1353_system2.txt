A school bus carrying 35 students and four staff members to a private school caught fire near the Huda City Centre Metro station on Monday morning. 
The children were rescued in time and no injuries were reported, however, the bus was badly burnt in the incident.The incident happened at 8.05 am when the bus attached with Amity International School in sector-43, Gurgaon was carrying students to school. 
There were four staff members in the bus who immediately started de-boarding the students, who were sent to the school in another bus.
