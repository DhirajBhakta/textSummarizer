"I decided, having taken an off of four years, I wanted to start off with slightly smaller roles to get into that zone. 
But I was sure I wont do it by compromising on the quality of work. 
But there has never been a point in my life where I wanted to take things slow, or I dont want to work. 
I have been working nonstop since I was 19 so not working is torturous. 
We may have had only 20 days of work but the impact (of our characters) is huge," says Akshaye, who is playing an investigative officer in the movie.
