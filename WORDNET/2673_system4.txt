Almost a week after the rollout of the landmark Goods and Services Tax (GST), the Congress today called it "imperfect" and a "mockery" of the original draft, and added that it should have been delayed by at least two months since it fails to achieve its purpose in its current form."
The GST Network should have undergone a dry run before implementation," former Finance Minister P Chidambaram said in a press conference in New Delhi."
There must be a trial run for two months to allow the industry and companies to clear their stocks and then a proper rollout should have happened from September," he said.On the Jammu and Kashmir Assembly passing the GST Bill, Chidambaram said it does not dilute Article 370.
