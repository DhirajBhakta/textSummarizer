Hospital, reported its first cadaver donation this month after the family of a  38-year-old brain-dead patient from Jalgaon, Sangita Mahajan, donated her body.The liver from the cadaver was transplanted to a 68-year-old end-stage organ failure patient at Jupiter Hospital,Thane, while the corneas were retained by JJ Hospital for transplants. 
After treatment for three days at a local hospital, she slipped into coma, and her relatives moved her to Sir JJ Hospital for further treatment. ?
The doctors were so excited that even head of the departments of concerned specialties, along with their teams were present throughout the night till the donation process was complete.
