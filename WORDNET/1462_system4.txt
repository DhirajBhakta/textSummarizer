Priyanka Chopra, now a formidable public voice among female entertainers in the US, tweeted her support to the hundreds of thousands of women who marched together in Washington on Sunday.The women's march saw more than 5,00,000 participants coming together in Washington to become a part of the first major act of organised protest against US President Donald Trump since he got sworn in as the 45th President of the United States of America.So proud of all my sisters and the men that are at the #WomensMarch I'm so upset I couldn't go. 
She also added that she was upset that she couldn't join.The official website of Women's March on Washington wrote in its mission statement that the march "will send a bold message to our (America's) new government on their first day in office, and to the world that women's rights are human rights."
