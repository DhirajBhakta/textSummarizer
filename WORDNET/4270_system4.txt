New Delhi, Mar 10 (PTI) The Income Tax Department has received responses from 8.38 lakh persons out of the 18 lakh SMS/email queries it had sent to 17.92 lakh people for suspicious deposits post demonetisation. 
"More than 12 lakh responses have been received from 8.38 lakh distinct PANs/persons which are under verification," Minister of State for Finance Santosh Kumar Gangwar said in a written reply in the Lok Sabha. 
He said an appropriate action in non-compliant cases is taken as per law, which includes searches, surveys, assessment of income, levy of taxes, penalties and filing of prosecution complaints in criminal courts. 
In a separate reply to a question in the Lok Sabha, Gangwar said during April 1, 2014 to December 31, 2016, the I-T department conducted searches in 1,838 groups of assessees, seizing undisclosed assets worth Rs 2,607 crore.
