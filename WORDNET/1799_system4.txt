Mumbai BJP president Ashish Shelar has raised questions over the appointment of Bollywood actor Salman Khan as the brand ambassador of Brihan Mumbai Municipal Corporation's (BMC) open defecation free (ODF) drive. 
While questioning the move he also raised the issue of a Humboldt penguin's death in Mumbai zoo."
Those who killed a penguin have appointed a brand ambassador who killed a black buck, it really amuses me..." 
The actor through his NGO Being Human Foundation had submitted a proposal to work towards an open defecation free Mumbai.The actor also pledged to donate mobile toilet vans to the civic body but Shiv Sena's move hasn't gone down well with the party's ally BJP.BJP QUESTIONS THE MOVE
