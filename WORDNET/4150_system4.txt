The driver, bodyguard and Ashok Yadav were hit multiple times, ADG RK Malik said.The four men were rushed to a local hospital but none could survive.The police recovered at least 50 AK-47 bullet shells from the crime spot.The police have launched a massive manhunt in Dhanbad and adjoining areas to nab the assailants. 
An SIT has been formed to probe the incident.Meanwhile, Chief Minister Raghubar Das has directed the DGP to maintain law and order in Dhanbad following the incident and asked him to nab the attackers as soon as possible.
