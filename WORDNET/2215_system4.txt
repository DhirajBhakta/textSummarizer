The Drama Company premiered on Sunday on Sony TV. 
Actor-comedian Krushna Abhishek is making a return to the small screen with this show. 
The show also features former TKSS members like Sugandha Mishra, and Ali Asgar, apart from other talented artists like Sanket Bhosle and Sudesh Lehri. 
The show will be aired on the same channel as The Kapil Sharma Show and comparisons between the two are inevitable.?
