A livid Chawrasia termed the shoddy treatment by IOA officials at the Rio Games as if they were "servants".It has been four months and they have had several paperworks done but Lahiri, a winner of 16 titles around the world, was not even paid a penny, while Chawrasia himself managed to get Rs 5.5 lakh so far, Chawrasia told reporters."
We don't like to keep on harping on this subject as we want to concentrate on the tougher assignments ahead," he added.Highlighting the payment issue, he said the sports ministry in a written communication had promised the amount of Rs 30 lakh towards their expense incurred in Rio and also for the coaching camps they attended in the build-up to the Olympics.
