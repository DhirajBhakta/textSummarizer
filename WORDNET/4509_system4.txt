Mumbai, Feb 23 (PTI) Fruit juice concentrate maker Rasna today said it is eyeing a revenue of Rs 250 crore in the next three years from the snack category. 
"We are looking at 5 per cent market share of a Rs 5,000 crore market that is growing at 15-16 per cent in the next three years. 
We are looking at Rs 250 crore from this category in the next three years," Rasna Chairman and Managing Director Piruz Khambatta told reporters here. 
The company has invested Rs 100 crore so far on Vitos and plans to export the snack brand to the neighbouring countries this year.
