Desperate to augment revenue, the cash-strapped Mamata Banerjee government in West Bengal has decided to enter the liquor distribution business, a move that is sure to raise a few eyebrows.In a precursor to the step, the government has almost done away with dry days in the state by allowing bars in three-star hotels and above to serve liquor for 365 days in a year. 
The government issued a notification in August last year in which it reduced the number of dry days observed from 12 to four.
