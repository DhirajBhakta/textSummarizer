This is one of the four cases registered in 2011 against a total of 120 Somali pirates, for holding 91 people from different countries hostage. 
The sessions court pronounced the verdict in the first of the four cases, where the pirates mother vessel carrying heavy arms and ammunitions was intercepted in 2011, and the pirates were caught.The coast guard and navy, in a joint operation, then rescued the 22 men being held hostage on the ship.
