AK Walia, veteran Congress leader, has quit the party alleging that tickets for upcoming MCD elections were being sold for money. 
Walia, who held several major portfolios in the Sheila Dikshit government in Delhi, claimed that some of the tickets were given to 'outsiders' in his constituency.
