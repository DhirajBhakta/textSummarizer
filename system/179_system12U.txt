Several first copies of the high-end watches were seized by the Directorate of Revenue Intelligence  at cargo complex of Delhis Indira Gandhi International Airport (IGIA). 
The consignment also carried huge quantity of cameras, sunglasses and mobile accessories worth Rs 10 crore.Original Rado watches cost over a lakh and the copes seized by DRI were meant to be sold in the market for Rs 4,000-5,000.
