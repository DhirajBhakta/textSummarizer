By Komal Panchmatia New York, Jul 14 (PTI) Salman Khan has never been a favourite at award shows but the superstar has no complains as he said he prefers performing at such ceremonies over receiving an honour. 
I dont go and receive awards, I just go and perform." 
Its like I am getting money for standing there," he said. 
And if I can get my act correct, which I always get after the concert, I promise IIFA will be fabulous and incredible.
