In a well-disposed move, the Border Security Force (BSF) today handed over a 15-year-old boy on humanitarian grounds to Pakistan authorities after he strayed to the Indian territory unknowingly. 
Mai was apprehended by BSF personnel on Friday, when he crossed over the International Border (IB), BSF spokesperson RK Kataria (Punjab Frontier) said.Up till now in 2017, the BSF has handed over six more Pakistan nationals who had crossed over.
