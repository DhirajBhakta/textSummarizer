The brewery's founder, Fredrik Tunedal, told VICE that he "wanted to optimise the experience of having beer in the shower by trying to customize a brew for this glorious occasion."
As a brewer I work long and hard days, and when I come home I'm often covered in malt dust (my girlfriend actually calls me Mr Malty Pants) and the shower is a gateway back to normal society."
This drink has created quite a wave of excitement among beer lovers, it seems to fulfill many demands.
