The National Democratic Alliances (NDA) presidential candidate Ram Nath Kovind visited Mumbai on Saturday for a brief campaign stop, ahead of Mondays election. 
Kovind spoke to MPs and legislators from the BJP and its allies in the state, including Shiv Sena, at Churchgates Garware Club.
