The 24th Battalion of the BSF has confiscated a bag of counterfeit notes of the new Rs 2000 notes worth Rs 6.96 lakhs in an ambush late on Monday night.The security agency carried out the operation at Gate Number 179 at the Chori-Anantapur border observation post (BOP) based on specific intelligence inputs. 
The BSF has now launched a joint probe into the case along with the Malda police and other central agencies.INDO-BANGLADESH BORDER- HUB OF FAKE CURRENCYThe raid comes just three days after the district police arrested a youth with fake currency notes worth Rs 1 lakh.
