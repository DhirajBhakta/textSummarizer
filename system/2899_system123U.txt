Bollywood's Hero No 1 is in Anurag Basu's Jagga Jasoos, and a latest still from the film is a proof of it. 
After his dud Aa Gaya Hero, Govinda will be seen in Ranbir Kapoor and Katrina Kaif-starrer which is set to hit the screens on July 14. 
If reports are to be believed, the actor will essay the role of a detective who will help Ranbir in finding his father in the film.
