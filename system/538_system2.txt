Ruling AIADMK on Monday said it would take legal action against senior Karnataka police official D Roopa if she did not withdraw her charges that party general secretary VK Sasikala was provided special treatment in prison, contravening laws.AIADMK mouthpiece Namadhu Dr MGR quoted the partys Perambur MLA and district secretary P Vetrivel and partys Karnataka state unit secretary VA Pugazhendi as saying that legal actions would be taken if Roopa did not take back her false charges.As DIG (Prison) a few days ago, Roopa had alleged that Sasikala was provided amenities flouting prison rules.
