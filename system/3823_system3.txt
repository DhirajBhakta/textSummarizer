The BJP's performance in Assembly by-election ahead of the crucial MCD polls has the Delhi unit chief Manoj Tiwari beaming with confidence. 
Taking a dig at both the Congress as well as the AAP, Tiwari said the people of Delhi have now decided not to be hoodwinked by false promises.He also took a dig at Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal for questioning the Electronic Voting Machines (EVM). 
AAP's Harjeet Singh failed to garner even one-sixth of the total votes polled.Despite the humiliating loss, Delhi Deputy CM Manish Sisodia expressed confidence of winning the upcoming MCD polls. 
Sisodia admitted that there was anger among people after AAP's MLA Jarnail Singh was asked to leave contest Punjab Assembly election."
