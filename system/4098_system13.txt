Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal promised on Saturday to abolish residential house tax in the Capital if his party is voted to power in the upcoming city civic polls.Kejriwal said the Aam Aadmi Party has worked out the mathematics and the money saved from corruption would be used to cover the shortfall after scrapping the house tax.Municipal elections in Delhi are due on April 23 and the AAP is contesting the polls for the first time.
