The fire affected train services for about 15 minutes, after which normal operations resumed.The Delhi Fire Services received the first call of the incident at 4pm responding to which it sent three fire tenders to Patel Nagar station. 
The train was moved to Kirti Nagar station as a precaution as there is an extra track available there that wouldnt affect normal metro operations, a fire official said.However, the same train caught fire again while being parked at Kirti Nagar station.
