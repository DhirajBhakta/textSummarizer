Twelve-year-old Aarey resident Aniket Dileep Page was attacked by a leopard near the Aarey pump house at Film City around 5.30pm on Friday. 
The forest department set up two cages on Thursday at two separate locations  near Film City and at the Aarey helipad  to trap the leopard. 
On their way back, as his friends walked ahead, Page bent down to scratch his leg, said Sameer Inamdar, forest officer, Mumbai range.
