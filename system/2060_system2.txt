Police on Wednesday raided a real estate agents house in southwest Delhis Chhawla area and recovered close to Rs 64 lakh in old and new currency and jewellery worth over Rs 1 crore.On the basis of a tip-off, a joint action team of income tax (I-T) and inter state crime cell of crime branch carried out a raid at the house of Sukhbir Shokeen, police said.Unaccounted cash amounting to Rs 64,84,000 was found.
