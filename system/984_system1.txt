It's been a week since Dileep was arrested on charges of criminal conspiracy and the actor has moved the Kerala High Court for a bail.On Monday, the Kerala police questioned Congress MLA Anwar Sadath of Aluva and CPI-M MLA-actor Mukesh in connection with the actress assault case.According to reports, Anwar Sadath was asked about his connections with actor Dileep. 
Anwar told the investigation team that he did not know Pulsar Suni, the prime accused in the case.On the other hand, actor-MLA Mukesh was questioned by the police team as Pulsar Suni was his driver for a year. 
Mukesh told the investigation team that he knew Suni very well.
