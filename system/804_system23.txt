A three-year-old girl who had gone missing on July 11, was thrown into a canal by her father to become a tantrik, police have claimed. 
The childs body was recovered from the canal in Faridabad the next day.The police had registered a case of kidnapping when Dheeraj Kumar of Pyali Chowk area under the Kotwali police station informed the police control room (PCR)that his daughter had been kidnapped.
