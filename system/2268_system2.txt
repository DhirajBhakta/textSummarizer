A person was caught in camera allegedly strangling a street dog to death with his feet in Noidas Sector 45 on Friday night.The man, who was allegedly drunk, stood with his feet pressing the dogs neck and mouth onto the road for about an hour and only moved after the dog breathed its last.The 18-sec video clip shot by a passerby shows the man standing on the dog, pressing the dogs mouth and neck onto the ground with one foot and holding its hind legs with another foot.One of the eyewitnesses, who shot the video in his cell phone camera, told Hindustan Times, that man was heavily drunk and pressed the dog to the ground with his feet for about an hour, before it died.
