Condemning the recent attacks on their students in Greater Noida, a group of African envoys have accused India of taking no action against the violence, which they said was "xenophobic and racial" in nature."
India took no action to prevent violence against Africans. 
It didn't properly condemn the Greater Noida attack," the African envoys said in a statement. 
"No known, sufficient and visible deterring measures were taken," it added.Calling the attacks "xenophobic and racial", the envoys said they expect strong condemnation and action from India.
