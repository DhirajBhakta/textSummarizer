Two murder accused  shot dead an 18-year-old witness in northwest Delhis Mahendra Park on Sunday night.Police said the victim, Jeet, was returning home with his brother, Ajeet, after buying groceries when he was confronted by two men. 
The men, both accused in a murder case and had been absconding, stopped Jeet, snatched the groceries and before he could react, shot at him.The bullet first hit Jeet in the arm and he fell on the ground.
