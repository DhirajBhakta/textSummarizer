Section 11 of the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 has provision for a maximum fine of Rs 50 and punishment of three months only.Also, Section 429 of the Indian Penal Code, which regards killing and maiming of an animal, gives a maximum punishment of five years that has so far not been given to anyone.Amita Singh, an animal rights activist and JNU professor, said, "Our whole struggle comes to a naught when these people simply get a bail and escape with a fine of just Rs 50."
