A 66-year-old from South West Delhis Dwarka Sector 22 allegedly committed suicide on Tuesday and in a note requested his organs be donated to All India Institute of Medical Sciences.He thanked doctors and police officials in advance.In his suicide note, the deceased, who has been identified as Prahlad Narayan Mishra, said he is tired of life.The man committed suicide in his apartment by using a cloth to hang himself from a ceiling fan, a senior police official said.
