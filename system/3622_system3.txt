In a freak incident, which delayed the Air India flight by an hour, a catering van hit the door of an ATR aircraft at Delhis Indira Gandhi International Airport (IGIA) on Sunday morning. 
Air India officials are assessing the damage and said that the loss will run into lakhs and the aircraft will also remain grounded till it is repaired.The catering vehicle was returning after loading the food in the aircraft. 
The passengers were shifted to a different aircraft.The vehicle hit the service gate and it is completely damaged now. 
Till the door is repaired, we will manage the operations by tweaking the schedule, the official added.The vehicle was of a ground handling company.
