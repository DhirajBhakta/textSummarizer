The manager of the Kotak Mahindra Bank branch on Kasturba Gandhi Marg was remanded in five-day custody of the Enforcement Directorate today by a Delhi district court in Saket.The agency had arrested the manager in connection with cases on hawala trader Paras Mal Lodha and Delhi-based lawyer Rohit Tandon. 
The bank branch came under the Income Tax department scanner in connection with two suspicious accounts.
