The civic body has formed Special 360 to tackle dengue in the most vulnerable pockets of Mumbai. 
Officials said the team will visit areas with highest dengue cases across 24 wards and will study data compiled in the past three years to contain dengue menace in the city.
Hence, we created a special team to focus on problem areas, destroy repeated breeding grounds and create awareness among people to keep their surroundings clean, said Rajan Naringrekar, insecticide officer, BMC.
