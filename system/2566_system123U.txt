West Bengal State Transport Corporation is selling off its antique trams. 
According to the last count WBSTC possessed some 269 tram cars. 
Of these at least 50 are antique, wooden models and most are no longer in working order. 
Out of these the corporation has short listed some 12 trams for sale. 
The first tram has been sold to a bidder from Chandigarh, who reportedly wants to refurbish it into a restaurant.
