Police arrested eight people on Friday for allegedly stealing about 5 crore litres of crude oil from Cairn Indias oilfield in Barmer over the last several years.Cairn India produces about 1.75 lakh barrels of oil per day from its Barmer oilfield, which is Indias largest onshore block. 
The company said it had filed a case and was cooperating with the police.The eight arrested were mainly transporters and some staff of Cairn contractor companies.
