The national blind cricket team which won the T-20 World Cup for Blind earlier this month met Prime Minister Narendra Modi here.Modi complimented the team members for their achievement and urged them to do even better in future, an official statement said.The team members presented the Prime Minister with an autographed bat, a ball and a team jersey with his name on it. 
Best wishes to the entire team that won the T-20 Cricket World Cup for the Blind.
