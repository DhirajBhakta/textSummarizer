From K J M Varma Beijing, Dec 21 (PTI) A school principal in China was today suspended for holding exams in open, defying closure orders and a red alert issued after the worst smog of the year that engulfed several parts of the country. 
Emergency measures such as car restrictions based on even- odd license plate numbers will end, and kindergartens and schools will resume classes, state-run Xinhua news agency reported. 
Meanwhile, the principal of a middle school in central Chinas Henan Province has been suspended from duty in Linzhou City for defying the orders to close all kindergartens and schools in the city following red alert for air pollution.
