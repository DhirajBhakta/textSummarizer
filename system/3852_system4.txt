Raju (24) was stabbed 18 times after he refused to serve golgappe to the five accused, police said. 
During the interrogation, the five reportedly told the police that they were drunk and got enraged when Raju refused to serve them golgappe. 
Police said when Raju reached I-Block, five men consuming alcohol stopped him and asked him to serve golgappe. 
The doctors said Raju suffered 18 stab injuries that punctured his vital organs, police said.A case of murder was registered at the Mangolpuri police station and three teams were formed to nab the attackers, who were suspected to be from the same locality.
