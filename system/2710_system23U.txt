New Delhi, Jul 3 (PTI) Only 16 per cent of terrorists succeeded in crossing the Line of Control (LoC) this year to enter Jammu and Kashmir till May 31. 
An Home Ministry official claimed today said 115 terrorists had made attempts to cross the LoC along the Jammu and Kashmir of which only 19 managed to sneak in. 
The data on infiltration shows that 119 terrorists had sneaked into the country in 2016.
