Despite being in the news for her rumoured relationship with Rock On 2 actor Farhan Akhtar, the 30-year-old actor says that she is happy focussing on her work. 
In an interview to Filmfare, Shraddha said, "I'm happy being single and focussing on my work. 
If you want to have a relationship, you will manage to balance it with work," she added. 
Shraddha's love life has made headlines in the recent past and the actor revealed the rumours of her dating Farhan have affected her in a certain way, but now she has learnt to ignore them. 
On the work front, she will be seen romancing Arjun Kapoor in Half Girlfriend.
