 A five-year-old girl was allegedly raped by a 55-year-old man in southeast Delhis Taimoor Nagar area on Monday afternoon. 
Police said the accused has been identified as Mukhtar Sheikh, a resident of West Bengal. 
The incident took place around 2 pm in the slum locality when Sheikh took the girl to her home on the pretext of chocolate. 
Police said the accused and girls parents live nearby.
