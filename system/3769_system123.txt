Veteran actor Amol Palekar filed a PIL with Supreme Court questioning the censorship laws that have not been contested for over 47 years now. 
The 72-year-old actor has sought relaxation in the censorship of films by means of removal of pre-censorship of movies. 
The Supreme Court has now issued a notice to the censor board asking them to file a reply to Palekar's plea.Palekar has been missing from silver screen for quite some time now.
