New Delhi, Feb 12 (PTI) Leading stock exchange NSE has proposed a pay package of Rs 8 crore per annum for its new chief Vikram Limaye and will seek approval of its shareholders on March 7 for his appointment and remuneration package. 
The fixed pay would include basic salary, house rent allowance, special allowance, leave travel allowance, medical allowance, lunch subsidy, contribution to provident fund, gratuity and superannuation and pension schemes.
