Jaipur, Dec 17 (PTI) The Department of Telecom (DoT) is set to launch a portal - Tarang - with data on all mobile towers and transmitters across the country in a month that will allow a user to check the radiation compliance status. 
Those who...want to get the radiation level checked of towers will be done by the government and he will be informed," Union Minister of State for Communications Manoj Sinha told reporters here. 
"We conduct test on random basis and through this portal and on payment, test of specific tower would be done," he added. 
Addressing an awareness programme on EMF emissions and telecom towers, the minister said, there are myths about the radiation and its impact on health which need to be eliminated by creating awareness.
