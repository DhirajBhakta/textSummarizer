Mumbai, Apr 16 (PTI) Actress Alia Bhatt, who played the role of a Bihari migrant in "Udta Punjab", said the film was not made with the intention of winning a National award. 
Abhishek Chaubeys "Udta Punjab", which dealt with the issue of substance abuse in Punjab, failed to win an award in the 64th National Film Awards. 
When asked how she felt on the film not getting recognition at the National awards, Alia said she did not feel bad, as the film had already been well received by the audience.
