A Public Interest Litigation in? Bombay High Court seeks the scrapping of Maharashtra government's much touted Shivaji statue in Arabian Sea. 
The statue is expected to be built at the cost of Rs 3,600 crore.The PIL has been filed by Mohan Bhide, a chartered accountant and a professor by profession who runs Bhide Kapasi classes in Mumbai. 
The project of constructing a statue in the middle of the sea is nothing but a political stunt of all the political parties operating in Maharashtra."
