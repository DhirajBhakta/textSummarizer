Floyd Mayweather Jr and Conor McGregor met face to face on Tuesday for the first time, at least publicly, as they kicked off a four-city, three-country international press tour to drum up interest in their 12-round super welterweight matchup on 26 August at T-Mobile Arena in Las Vegas.Both men lived up  or down, depending on your perspective  to their reputations as two of the most profane, outspoken trash-talkers in all of sports.
