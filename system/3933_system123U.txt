A judge in Los Angeles has rejected Oscar-winning filmmaker-actor Roman Polanski's plea to resolve his 40-year-old rape case.Polanski's plea was heard on Monday, reports variety.com.In a 13-page ruling, judge Scott Gordon found that Polanski cannot put the case behind him so long as he refuses to return to the US and submit to the court's jurisdiction."
Polanski cannot avail himself of the court while standing in contempt of it," Gordon wrote.California courts have repeatedly held that Polanski cannot litigate the case from afar.The famed director fled the US in 1978 after pleading guilty to raping a 13-year-old girl.
