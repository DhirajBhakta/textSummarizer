The film which is currently being shot in Austria has on board a full-fledged Hollywood stunt-and-action crew led by X-Men and The Dark Knight stunt director Tom Struthers.According to recent reports, this coming week, Tiger Zinda Hai star Salman Khan will be shooting an action scene where he will have to fight off a pack of wolves.An insider close to the film's production told DNA, "It is producer Aditya Chopra's vision that the film matches with an international standards of action and the crew is going the extra mile to ensure that the spy drama Tiger Zinda Hai delivers on that vision. 
Four years prior to Sultan, Salman starred in Kabir Khan's spy thriller Ek Tha Tiger, produced by Yash Raj Films. 
So, obviously, there are a lot of expectations from Ek Tha Tiger's sequel Tiger Zinda Hai, starring Salman Khan and helmed by the Sultan director Ali Abbas Zafar.The source further added, "The international action crew is taking all cautionary measures to ensure the wolves are taken care of by their trainers, who have been working with the team for several days now, and the action sequence will follow all guidelines."
