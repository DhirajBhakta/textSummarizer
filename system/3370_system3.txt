Taking lesson from the killing of the 22-year-old Army officer Lieutenant Ummer Fayaz in Shopian, the Indian Army has directed all soldiers to strictly follow guidelines of informing the local units before going home on leave in the Valley.? Sources in the security establishment said that there is a guideline which local officers or soldiers need to follow in the Valley. 
There is an existing direction to all heads of Army units across the state to ensure that no soldier/officer leaves his unit until there is a proper go ahead in terms of safety," said a source in the Army.
