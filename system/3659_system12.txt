A Kashmiri project fellow at BITS Pilani in Rajasthan has left the institute after allegedly facing harassment. 
Hashim Sofi, a 27-year-old scholar who was at BITS as a research project staff, has left for his hometown, Bandipora in Kashmir. 
Even Union Home Minister Rajnath Singh had expressed concern over the issue of security of Kashmiri students across the country and had appealed all te state governments to take action to ensure safety of students.
