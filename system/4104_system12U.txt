Being dubbed as the youngest father in India is a 12-year-old boy has been booked under the Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act for impregnating 17-year-old girl in Kerala.A report on The Hindu says that DNA tests have confirmed that the boy is indeed the biological father. 
Doctors believe that the boy could have a medical condition called "precocious puberty" which make the child attain puberty earlier in age.The identities of the young parents and the baby are being kept secret as per the POCSO Act.
