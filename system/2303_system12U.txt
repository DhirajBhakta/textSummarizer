A man has been arrested for allegedly using forged letters of External Affairs minister Sushma Swaraj to get heli-tickets to the Mata Vaishno Devi cave shrine, police said in Jammu on Saturday.The accused, Sandeep, sold these tickets in the black market in Reasi district of Jammu and Kashmir, they said.Police has solved the forgery case regarding selling of heli-tickets to the cave shrine of Mata Vaishnodevi in black market.
