Inordinate delay of a SpiceJet flight SG 143 resulted in a face-off between passengers and the airline crew on Tuesday. 
Scores of passengers including children and elderly were made to sit inside the flight for hours, claims passengers.The crew allegedly could not provide any reason for the delay. 
The flight was scheduled to take off at 8.55 pm but got delayed to 9.15 pm and then by another 15 minutes.
