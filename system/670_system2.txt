TruJet, which is operating services at Nanded airport, has started a Nanded-Hyderabad flight, and plans to start a Mumbai sector by September, said Valsa Nair Singh, the principal secretary in charge of civil aviation in the state government. 
The next stage of bidding is scheduled in September, Singh said.The state will also provide security and fire services to the airports free of charge, give a concession on water charges and provide any land required for expansion free of charge.
