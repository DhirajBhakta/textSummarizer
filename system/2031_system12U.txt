Finance Minister Arun Jaitley has said that demonetisation is the biggest step taken against black money and wrongful economic practices in the country in the last 70 years.Jaitley said that only a minor segment of parliamentarians are opposing the demonetisation move, which will bring far more greater long term benefits for the country.Finance Minister Arun Jaitley was speaking at a function at FICCI in New Delhi.
