His body was crushed after the elevator suddenly moved and trapped his leg. 
The police later arrested two bouncers who did not help the victims and even dissuaded the police from breaking the elevators glass.On April 22 this year, an elevator at Central Park-2 on Sohna road fell to the lower basement with a family reportedly stuck inside.
