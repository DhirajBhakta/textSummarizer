In yet another announcement, Parrikar also said that the number of off shore casinos in Mandovi river would be capped to existing five vessels, which?would be shifted to land in next three years. 
"The sixth off shore casino can go somewhere else and can operate only if people of the area dont have a problem," he said. 
The off shore casinos will be given an option to move to land in the special entertainment zone where Goans would be banned from entering," he said.
