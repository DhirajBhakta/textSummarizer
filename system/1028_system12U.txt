The two biggest taxi unions of the city have opposed the plan for black and yellow cabs if it puts a financial burden on their members.Mumbai Taximens Union and Mumbai Taxi Association have opposed the plan to install GPS citing additional expenses. 
The unions, however, claimed that they had brought their own taxi booking app, through which most of their taxis could be tracked.There has not been a molestation in black and yellow taxis and therefore it is one of the safest transport modes for women, said AL Quadros, leader of Mumbai Taximens Union.
