Mumbai, Apr 12 (PTI) Supermodel Gigi Hadid is all set to visit India as part of her global #TogetherTour to celebrate her Spring 2017 TommyXGigi collaborative collection. 
"Travelling around the world to meet fans and present the TommyXGigi collections has been an amazing experience. 
I look forward to visiting Mumbai and celebrating our final #TogetherTour stop this season," Gigi said in a statement.
