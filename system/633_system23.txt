 Farmers outfits in Maharashtra will restart their protest to demand a blanked loan waiver and implementation of the Swaminathan commissions recommendations across the state by staging rasta roko on August 14. 
Raju Shetti, farmers leader and Swabhimani Paksha chief, said they will intensify the agitation if the government wont accept their demands after August 14.The awareness march was carried out through 26 districts and 14 big rallies were held.
