Hotelier Farhan Azmi approached the Mumbai police on Wednesday saying that he received death threats on the phone.Azmi is the son of Samajwadi Party (SP) MLA Abu Azmi. 
Azmi in his application claimed that the caller told him that he would suffer as he had married a Hindu woman.Police officers confirmed that Farhan had given them an application and also submitted the phone recordings of the threatening calls. 
The police said that they would now investigate the case.
