It is a sin to defame people, he added.Using striking terminology, Francis said journalists and the media must avoid falling into coprophilia  an abnormal interest in excrement. 
And since people have a tendency towards the sickness of coprophagia, a lot of damage can be done.He also spoke of the danger of using the media to slander political rivals. 
It is a sin and it is hurtful.Disinformation was the greatest potential harm the media could cause, he said, because it directs opinion in only one direction and omits the other part of the truth.
