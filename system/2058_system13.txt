In the wake of Income Tax raids being conducted across the country, the government today called it a "surgical strike" on illegally accumulated money.Addressing a press conference, Secretary, Economic Affairs, Shaktikanta Das today said, "The action taken by the enforcement agencies on illegally accumulated money is based on specific inputs, you may call it a surgical strike".Earlier, Prime Minister Narendra Modi had asked BJP members to stop calling demonetisation move "a surgical strike" on black money.
