A suspected mortar shell was found at Vasant Kunj in South Delhi early on Saturday, triggering panic in the locality.A ragpicker alerted local residents after discovering the explosive at a garbage dumping site in Kishangarh area. 
The police control room was informed around 8.30 am.Additional deputy commissioner of police (south) Chinmoy Biswal said personnel from the nearest police station  led by station house officer Gagan Bhaskar  were immediately deployed to the area.
