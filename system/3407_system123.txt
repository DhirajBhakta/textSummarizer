American Sniper actor Sienna Miller is the latest celebrity to fall victim to online hackers and have her nude photos circulated all over the internet. 
According to a report in The Sun, the hackers may have targetted the actor and/or her friends' cloud storage accounts, social media sites or messaging services to get access to the photos.The report quoted a source as saying, "Sienna will be devastated that these pictures said to be of her fooling around have been put online by some weirdo."
