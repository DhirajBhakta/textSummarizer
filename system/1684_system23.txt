Ruling Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), opposition Congress and new entity Aam Aadmi party (AAP) makes it triangular battle in Goa.Weeks before the poll AAP Chief and Delhi Chief Minister Arvind kejriwal kick starts his poll campaign in Goa today. 
During his poll campaign escalating charges against ruling BJP govt in Goa and the Chief Minister Lakshmikant Parsekar, kejriwal said, Parsekar represents corrupt poltics. 
Rebuking the opponents over putting light weight candidates, Kejriwal said AAP candidates are not political light weights because they will defeat established politicians soon in Goa too as they did in delhi.
