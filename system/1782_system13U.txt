You don't need to spend a fortune on cabs everyday to reach office regularly now, espeically if you commute in Noida and Ghaziabad.The regional transport authority has final given a green signal to bike taxis in four of UP districts that fall under Ghaziabad transport region. 
The services will be available in Ghaziabad, Gautam Budh Nagar, Hapur and Bulandshahr.The bike taxi or baxi service is intended to boost last-mile connectivity options as well.
