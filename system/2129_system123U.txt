When Kaun banega Crorepati first started airing in the year 2000, the whole nation turned towards the small screen at 9pm sharp--just to get a glimpse of the legend that is Mr. 
Amitabh Bachchan, interacting with the common man or woman.That has been the kind of magic the Indian megastar managed to weave on television. 
The show became immensely popular with the Indian masses, and only a part of it was owed to the money we could win.
