Official say there are 2.05 lakh streetlights, semi-mast and high-mast lights installed at roads and parks under the North corporation. 
Out of this, the contract has been awarded for 1.15 lakhs light fixtures in two bigger zones  Rohini and Narela   in phase I.For installation and execution, the civic body has appointed consortium of Havells India Ltd. and Tata Power Delhi Distribution Ltd .
