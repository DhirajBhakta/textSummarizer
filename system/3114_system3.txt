Misa Bharti, daughter of former Bihar Chief Minister Lalu Prasad, has been summoned notice by the Income Tax department in connection with a benami land deal case. 
WHAT IS THE CASEA chartered accountant, Rajesh Kumar Agrawal, allegedly linked to Bharti and others was also arrested by the Enforcement Directorate (ED) on May 22. 
I-T department officials had said they will apply provisions of the newly enacted Benami Transactions Act, 1988, which became operational from November 1 last year, in this case.
