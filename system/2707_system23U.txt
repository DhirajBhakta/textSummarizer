Swashbuckling opener Chris Gayle has been included in the 13-man West Indies team which will play a T20 international match against India in Jamaica on Sunday, July 9.The left-hander is the highest run-maker for the Windies in the T20 format with 1,519 runs (average 35.32, strike rate 145.49), including two centuries. 
He last played a T20 International for West Indies in April, 2016, in the World T20 final vs England at Kolkata's Eden Gardens."
