Tamil television actress Nandini's husband Karthikeyan had reportedly committed suicide at a lodge in Virugambakkam, Chennai on Monday (April 3). 
According to IBT, Karthikeyan consumed poison with cold drinks. 
Reports also suggest that Karthikeyan, who owned a gym, blamed his father-in-law for his suicide.?
