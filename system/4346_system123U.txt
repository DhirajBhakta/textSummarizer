The International Cricket Council is planning to send a World XI to Pakistan in late September for a four-match Twenty20 series in Lahore that will look to build on the successful staging of the Pakistan Super League final in the city on Sunday.The four-match series at the Gaddafi Stadium  to be called The Independence Cup to mark the 70th anniversary of partition  is intended to continue the reintroduction of top-level cricket to the country after a near eight-year hiatus.
