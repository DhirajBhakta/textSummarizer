Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath today ordered an investigation into the Gomti riverfront project within 45 days. 
Adityanath had earlier expressed dissatisfaction over the progress of the Akhilesh Yadav government's Gomti riverfront project.The project was launched to channelise the river by constructing a diaphragm wall and by landscaping and constructing intercepting drains on both banks.? ? Adityanath was unhappy that although two years had lapsed, and over Rs 1,427 crore had been spent, only 60 per cent of the work was complete, an official release said.While interacting with officials and staff involved in the project, Adityanath said a demand of an additional amount of Rs 1,500 crore was being made for the project, which was initially scheduled to be completed by May 2017."
