School teacher Kailash Chandra says that he has been forced to take classes in the toilet because there is no school building. 
When the weather is fine in summer and winter, the classes are held under a tree, but now the earth is wet so I cannot hold the classes under a tree and am forced to use the toilet, he told HT.Kailash Chandra said that it is not as if the higher-ups have not been informed.
