The accused and the victim came in contact with each other around a year ago on matrimonial website, and duo started communicating.They exchanged mobile numbers and after few months, they decided to marry. 
Patel took the gold ring and picked up the phone to call someone and went out of the cafe, said senior inspector Ramchandra Jadhav of Khar police station.The victim was inside in the CCD but Patel did not return. 
After half an hour, the victim came out of the cafe to look for him and tried to call him, but his mobile number was switched off, said Jadhav.The woman then approached Khar police station and registered a case against Patel.
