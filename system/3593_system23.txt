Kumar Vishwas has hit out at Delhi Chief Minister and AAP convener Arvind Kejriwal over a range of issues.Speaking to India Today, Kumar Vishwas questioned Arvind Kejriwal's defence of AAP's loss in Punjab, Rajouri Assembly by-poll in Delhi and the MCD elections.Kumar Vishwas said that the party needs 'radical reforms'. 
The question of change of leadership will also be discussed in the party, Vishwas told India Today.
