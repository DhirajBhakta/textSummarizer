Mumbai, Dec 26 (PTI) Taking a dig at NCP president Sharad Pawar, Maharashtra Chief Minister Devendra Fadnavis today said those who harbour ambitions to become prime minister never get the top job. 
"The aspirations of those who wish to be the prime minister are never fulfilled. 
(So) let me remain chief minister," Fadnavis said during an interaction with IIT-Bombay students here.
