Malayalam media giant, Mathrubhumi has now decided to announce 'First Day of Period' leave for its employees. 
The leave policy will come into place immediately. 
The company's Joint Managing Director MV Shreyams Kumar was quoted as saying that the additional leave will be granted to its women employees which will "not be counted under sick leave or casual leave". 
The first day of period leave will be an additional leave for the women employees and will not be considered as casual leave or sick leave. 
We took the decision not because any employee asked for it, but felt that it should be granted," the MD said
