This wholesale cash & carry stores by @walmartindia will bring investment of 900 crore and generate 30,000 jobs in Maharashtra, the chief ministers office said in a series of tweets.Pravin Pardeshi, additional chief secretary at chief minister office, Sunil Porwal, additional chief secretary for industries, Harshdeep Kamble, development commissioner and Krish Iyer Walmarts India head were among others present at the time of signing of MoU.At present, the retail giant has two stores in the state  Aurangabad (Marathwada) and Amravati (Vidarbha).
