The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) is planning to postpone Class 12 board exams to March second week owing to the upcoming assembly polls in five states, sources said. 
As long as the last date of the examination held is maintained, there will be no problem, The Indian School principal, Tania Joshi, said. 
However, teachers said students shouldnt worry as CBSE usually holds the Humanities examination in the later part of April.
