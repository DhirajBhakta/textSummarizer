India's highly anticipated Baahubali: The Conclusion is facing stiff protest from an activist Vatal Nagaraj, who demanded Sathyaraj to apologise for his insensitive remarks on Kannadigas during the Cauvery water issue.Speaking about the issue, SS Rajamouli, in an exclusive interview with India Today, said, "It is a sad thing and we were shocked to see that on social media." 
When we started inquiring, we learned that whatever comments Mr Sathyaraj made it was nine years back.
