The Election Commission of India today asked the political parties to feel free to hack their Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs) this Sunday or Monday. 
The decision was taken at an all-party meeting the poll panel held today in New Delhi.The all-party meeting with the Election Commission was called after a number of them, including the Congress and the Aam Aadmi Party, alleged widespread tampering of the EVMs in the recent Assembly and municipal elections. ? Representatives of seven national parties and 48 state parties were invited by the poll panel for the meeting at the Constitution Club in Delhi to reassure the parties that the EVMs cannot be tampered with and were secured.
