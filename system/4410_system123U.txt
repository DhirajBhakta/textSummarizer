Reliance is having some technical troubles as it rolls out its Jio Prime offer to Jio users. 
After initially glitches yesterday, when the Jio Prime became available for subscription, and today, the company has temporarily halted the Jio Prime sign up process. 
In a message to users it hinted that the glitches are due to "load" on its servers.Update: It seems that Jio has fixed the problem it was having in the morning with its servers.
