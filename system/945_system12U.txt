As many as 73 roads with operational significance are being constructed along the Sino-India border, the Lok Sabha was informed onTuesday.The government has decided to undertake construction of 73 roads of operational significance along Indo-China border. 
Out of these 73 roads, 46 are being constructed by the Ministry of Defence and 27 by the Ministry of Home Affairs, Minister of State for Home, Kiren Rijiju, said in reply to a written question.He said 30 roads had been completed so far.
