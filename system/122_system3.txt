China's number one boxer and WBO Oriental Super Middleweight Champion Zulpikar Maimaitali has hit back at Vijender Singh ahead of Asia's biggest showdown 'Battleground Asia', saying he will show the Indian knockout king what "Chinese are capable of".Ahead of the bout, Vijender had trolled his Chinese opponent and said that he would try to finish the bout as soon as possible as "Chinese products don't last long".Replying to Vijender's comments Maimaitiali said, "I will show Vijender what Chinese are capable of. 
I will show what this kid is made of," he added.This fight between two top boxers of Asian subcontinent will decide the 'King of Asia'.China's undefeated star boxer has eight bouts under his name, same as Vijender.
