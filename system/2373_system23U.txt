Two persons, including a policeman, were arrested by the Gurgaon police for allegedly getting a gangster to make a threatening phone call for mediating in a property dispute in Wazirabad village on Wednesday. 
Police said  an inmate who was lodged in Bhondsi jail sought the help of gangster Manjeet Mahal to threaten the other group lead by Rakesh, which had claimed the plot on the upscale Golf Course Road.
