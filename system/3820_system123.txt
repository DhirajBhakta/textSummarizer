Munshi Venu, who's known for playing supporting roles in Malayalam films, passed away at a palliative care centre at Chalakudy, Thrissur. 
According to reports, the actor suffered kidney failure today. 
He was 63.The actor, was diagnosed with kidney-related ailments last year, had been undergoing dialysis since then. 
Reports also suggest that Munshi Venu didn't own a house and that he was living at a lodge in Chalakudy for the last 10 years.Making his acting debut with Kamal's comedy drama Pachakuthira, Munshi Venu will be more remembered for his comical characters.
