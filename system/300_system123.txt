Mumbai Congress chief Sanjay Nirupam has issued an ultimatum to Mumbai University administration and the Maharashtra government to declare university results on or before July 31, failing which party cadres and NSUI workers will gherao the Mumbai University campus on August 2.According to Congress, over 20 lakh answer sheets are yet to be checked by the university. 
Students, who had planned to go abroad for further education, are suffering due to this delay.The evaluation of answer sheets has got delayed because of a faulty online paper checking system which Mumbai University hired this year.
