Jammu, Jul 29 (PTI) Union minister Jitendra Singh today said the tricolour is "sacrosanct" and termed as "shocking and ridiculous" Chief Minister Mehbooba Muftis remarks that no one in Jammu and Kashmir would be there to hold the flag if their special rights are tinkered with. 
I have no doubt in saying that there will be no one to hold it (national flag) (if it is tinkered)."
