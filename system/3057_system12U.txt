A punter lost a whopping 70,000 pounds betting on a Tory majority, believing the election was "unlosable" for May. 
The Prime Minister was riding high with a seemingly unassailable double-digit lead over Labours Jeremy Corbyn in the polls and the only question on pundits minds seemed to be, "how big will the landslide be" Bookmakers hailed a bumper evening as large numbers of people placed big bets on the Conservatives to romp to a convincing victory which was not to be.
