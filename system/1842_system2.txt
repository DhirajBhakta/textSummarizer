A 26-year-old junior resident doctor of AIIMS who was found dead on July 10  in mysterious circumstances at his house in Gautam Nagar in South Delhi was possibly murdered.A murder case was registered on Saturday, over five months after police said Sarvanan Ganeshan had committed suicide by injecting a medicine in his right arm. 
They said it appears someone injected the medicine forcefully, a police source said.Sources said the bottle of medicine, suspected to be potassium chloride, was not found at the spot.
