Officials said they collected Rs20 lakh in fine from residents who failed to eliminate mosquito breeding spots in their homes.
?Rajan Naringrekar, insecticide officer, BMC, said the civic body focussed its efforts on finding breeding grounds of dengue carrier Aedes aegypti and malaria carrier Anopheles stephensi. 
Negligence could lead to breeding of 400-600 mosquitoes which carry dengue and malaria, said Naringrekar.
