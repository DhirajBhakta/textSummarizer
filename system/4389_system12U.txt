First phase of Manipur Assembly elections ended today, with over 80 per cent voter turnout being recorded till reports last came in. 
As many as 1,643 polling stations were set up in areas spread over Imphal East, Imphal West, Bishnupur and the hill districts of Churachandpur and Kangpokpi.More than 11 lakh voters exercised their franchise to decide the fate of 168 candidates in the fray.There was heavy security in parts of the state amid continual clashes with the United Naga Council.
