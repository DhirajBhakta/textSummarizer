A day after Prime Minister Narendra Modi attacked his predecessor Manmohan Singh with "taking bath while wearing a raincoat " jibe, the Congress has decided to boycott the Budget session in Parliament.The Congress has also sought an apology from the Prime Minister over the barb. 
The Congress and some other parties in Opposition had walked out of the Rajya Sabha on Wednesday after Narendra Modi's remark on Manmohan Singh. 
Later in the day, P Chidambaram started discussion on the Union Budget in the Rajya Sabha and called it "disappointing".
