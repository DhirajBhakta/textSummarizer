New Delhi, Apr 9 (PTI) Users of Airtel, Vodafone and Idea mobile services filed maximum billing complaints in October-December of 2016, as per the latest report of Trai. 
As for other quality parameters, Trai found performance of Aircel below par for call drops in most telecom circles. 
The penalty kicks in for more than 2 per cent call drops in a quarter in one telecom circle. 
Airtel, Sistema Shyam and Reliance Communications (GSM) service also overshot the call drop level in one circle each.
