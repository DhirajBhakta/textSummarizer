How tall were you when you were eightWell, for the Meerut-based Karan Singh it is a whopping 6 feet 6 inches. 
Yes, you heard it right.This 8-year-old was always different from the rest ever since he was born. 
He even made it to Guinness book of world records after his birth. 
He weighed 7.8 kg and was 63 cm in height at the time of birth which made him the heaviest and the tallest baby.Karan is now the world's tallest 8-year-old at 6ft 6in.
