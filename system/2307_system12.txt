A four-member panel comprising BCCI acting president CK Khanna, CEO Rahul Johri, Committee of Administrators (CoA) member Diana Edulji and acting secretary Amitabh Choudhary will take a call on the salary of the newly-appointed head coach Ravi Shastri.If news doing the rounds in BCCI corridors is to be believed then Shastri is expected to get Rupees seven crore per year for two years.
