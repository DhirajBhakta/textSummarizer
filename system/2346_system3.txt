Mithali Raj continued her magnificent form in ODI cricket as she slammed a magnificent century against New Zealand in a crucial ICC Womens World Cup encounter in Derby. 
It was her first  ODI century since 2014, when she hit an unbeaten 104 against Sri Lanka in Vizag.Mithali finally departed for 109 while Krishnamurthy slammed 70 off 45 balls as India ended up on a formidable 265/7 against New Zealand.Her sixth ODI century continued her record breaking run in ODIs in 2017. 
She also became the first cricketer to go past 6000 ODI runs.
