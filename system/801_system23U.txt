Ask actor Akshay Kumar and he will tell you how difficult it is. 
The 49-year-old actor, who is currently shooting for Reema Kagti's Gold in London, is also babysitting his daughter as wife Twinkle Khanna is touring Europe. 
And in an interview to Mumbai Mirror, the Rustom actor confessed that his respect for his wife has gone several notches higher. 
He told the daily, "My wife (Twinkle Khanna) is touring Europe, my son Aarav is in Oxford for a two-week study course so I'm babysitting my daughter Nitara besides shooting for a film (Reema Kagti's sports-drama Gold).
