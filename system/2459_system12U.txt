New Delhi, Jul 13 (PTI) Markets regulator Sebi has asked Goldmine Agro Ltd (GAL) and its directors to refund investors money that the Kolkata-based company had raised illegally by issuing securities. 
Besides, Sebi imposed a ban on the firm and its directors from accessing the capital markets for four years from the completion of refund. 
However, the company had not given details about the number of allottees.
