Los Angeles, Feb 24 (PTI) Rapper Kanye West is reportedly set to launch a new cosmetic line inspired by his late mother Donda West. 
The "Famous" hitmaker has filed legal docs declaring his intention to produce DONDA brand makeup, perfumes, lotion and other cosmetics, reported Ace Showbiz. 
According to insiders, 39-year-old Kanyes cosmetic line will also be for men.
