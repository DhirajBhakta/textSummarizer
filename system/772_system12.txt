Congress general secretary Ambika Soni has resigned as in-charge of the party's Uttarakhand and Himachal Pradesh units, citing health reasons. 
Soni handed over her resignation to the Congress high command. 
However, she has said that she wants to continue working as a Congress MP.Talking to ANI, Soni said, "I have requested party leadership to relieve me as in-charge of Uttarakhand and Himachal Pradesh due to health reasons."
