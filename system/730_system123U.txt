Now that his upcoming films Toilet: Ek Prem Katha and Padman bear a social message, hes being called the star that takes up causes. 
I dont want any particular image for myself, I dont want anybody to say that I am an action or a comedy hero. 
I want people to think ki ye koi bhi role kar leta hai (He can do any kind of a role), says the 49-year-old National Award winning actor, whose next film deals with the issue of open defecation, and propagates the message of Swachh Bharat Abhiyan in an entertaining way.
