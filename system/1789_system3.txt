According to a mind-blowing revelation by the Mumbai police, names of Bollywood actors are used by the Mumbai underworld to refer to various key terms in the drug supplying and peddling business.That the Mumbai underworld has always had code words for commonplace things is not news anymore. 
Initially, Mumbai police would be perplexed on hearing statements like "Sultan is always late" or "Sultan proved a box-office hit" over the phone.
