BJP MLA from Lauria, Vinay Bihari today reached the Bihar Assembly in most unusual way and state.When he reached the Bihar Assembly, he was bruised with injury marks on his knees.He was wearing vest and shorts as he entered the Assembly today.This was a kind of individual protest march by Vinay Bihari, who is highlighting non-construction of roads in his constituency.Vinay Bihari said, "Chief Minister Nitish Kumar had assured him to construct road in his area but till now nothing has been done and therefore he took to this unique mode of protest."
