Nisheeth Jha, the husband of Assamese actor Bidisha Bezbaruah who allegedly committed suicide by hanging herself from a ceiling fan, was on Thursday sent to 14 days in judicial custody. 
We are searching call details, Facebook, WhatsApp, and Twitter accounts of Jha to ascertain the reasons behind the victims extreme step, a senior police officer said.Jha was arrested on Tuesday night on charges of abetment to suicide after Bidishas father, Aswini Kumar Bezbaruah, accused him of having an extra-marital affair. 
Police said Jha was out of Gurugram when she killed herself. 
The couple earlier had an argument over Jhas relationship with the other woman.According to Assistant Commissioner of Police Harinder Singh, Bidisha had moved into the rented flat at Sushant Apartments on Sunday.Her body was discovered by a property dealer who along with some neighbours broke into her flat after Jha contacted him saying she was not responding to his phone calls, police said.
