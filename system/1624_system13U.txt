Shah Rukh Khan and Salman Khan's love-hate-love relationship has been in the news for a long time. 
From promoting each other's films to attending the parties together to praising each other on social media, the two Khans have done everything possible to display their affection for each other. 
So much so that Shah Rukh postponed the release date of Raees which was earlier supposed to clash with Salman Khan's Sultan.
