This year's India edition just wrapped up, with Rohan Rege from Paasha, JW Marriott Pune, as the winner. 
For those who aren't aware, mixology is both a science and an art--something only a handful of Indians have mastered till now. 
Rege, and the other 11 contestants were selected from a huge crop of 296 entrants this year (marking a participation increase of 41% from 2016).
