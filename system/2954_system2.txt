The Central Bureau of Investigation (CBI) team visited Delhi Health Minister Satyendra Jain's house today in connection with probe related to a money laundering case.According to reports, the CBI team sought clarifications from Satyendra Jain's wife regarding allegations of money laundering against the minister.The CBI reached there as per time given by Satyendra Jain's wife for certain clarifications in ongoing investigation in money laundering case.
