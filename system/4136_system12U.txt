New Delhi, Mar 22 (PTI) State-run Hudco has received capital markets regulator Sebis approval to raise funds through an initial share sale as part of the governments disinvestment drive. 
The initial public offer will comprise sale of 200,190,000 equity shares --10 per cent stake -- by the central government through an offer for sale (OFS) route, as per the Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP).
