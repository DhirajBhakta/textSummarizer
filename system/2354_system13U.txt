The anti-narcotics cell (ANC) of the Mumbai police crime branch arrested a 40-year-old woman selling ganja near a college at Churchgate station on Thursday. 
We are cracking down on peddlers operating near educational institutions as part of our campaign against drugs. 
Our men arrested Malan Venkatesh Kale, 40, while they were patrolling the area, said ANC chief DCP Shivdeep Lande.We were patrolling the area outside schools and colleges in the afternoon when we spotted Kale on Sipahi Molani Road.
