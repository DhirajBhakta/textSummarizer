The Los Angeles County coroner confirmed on Friday that Linkin Park frontman Chester Bennington died by hanging.Coroners office spokesperson Ed Winter says the 41-year-old rocker hanged himself from a bedroom door in his home near Los Angeles. 
Bennington was found dead Thursday. 
Winter says a half-empty bottle of alcohol was found in the room, but no drugs were evident. 
Bennington struggled with drug and alcohol addictions at various times during his life.
