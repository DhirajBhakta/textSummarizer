Samajwadi Party's candidate from Alapur Chandrashekhar Kanaujia died following a heart attack during an election campaign in Ambedkar Nagar.Kanaujia was campaigning ahead of the Assembly election in Uttar Pradesh. 
The 38-year-old SP candidate was campaigning in Sabitpur region of the constituency when he suffered a heart attack. 
He was rushed to a nearby hospital where he was declared brought dead.The Samajwadi Party had given Kanuajia the ticket against its current legislator Bhim Prasad Sonkar.
