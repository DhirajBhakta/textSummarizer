The police have arrested two people who opened nine accounts in the Naya Bazar branch of Kotak Mahindra Bank, in which they deposited R34 crore in scrapped banknotes. 
The bank, on its own, on observing the nature of transactions in these accounts, proactively filed a report with Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU) for further investigations, said Rohit Rao, spokesperson, Kotak Mahindra Bank.As per the instructions, the bank deposited the entire amount that was credited with these accounts with the Income Tax department.
