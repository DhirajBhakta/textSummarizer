Delhis new top bureaucrat has cracked the whip on lax officers, warning of strict action on babus who come to work after 9.45am, as part of a raft of tough new measures.Newly appointed chief secretary MM Kutty has broken away from his predecessor KK Sharmas low-profile style, and asked secretaries of every department to ensure punctuality after noticing that a large number of officers were often late.The general administration department needs to ensure all officers working in the Delhi secretariat mark their attendance through biometric system by 9:45 am, he told officers, as per the official minutes of the meeting.An official present at the meeting also said that Kutty asked secretaries of every department to take action against habitual late-comers.The 1985 batch IAS officer was working as additional secretary in the Union environment ministry and took charge on December 1.He had earlier served as principal secretary to former chief minister Sheila Dikshit and as finance secretary in the present Aam Aadmi Party administration.
