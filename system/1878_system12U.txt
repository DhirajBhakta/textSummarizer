Meet Yadireddy, a beggar from Andhra capital city Vijayawada, who donated a silver crown to Lord Ram.At a time when many across the country are facing a severe cash crunch, the offering made by the beggar to Lord Ram came as a surprise for many.75 year old Yadireddy hailing from Nalgonda district of Telangan had came to Vijayawada as teenager. 
During his life's journey for livelihood he did many odd jobs and drove a rickshaw for almost forty five years.
