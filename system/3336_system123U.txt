The book released earlier this year and the candid confessions in it by Karan Johar charmed readers and critics all across the country.However, as it turns out, Karan Johar may have lied about his family members in the book. 
According to a report in Indian Express, what Karan Johar has written about his father's family may be fiction and not fact.In An Unsuitable Boy, Karan Johar has written that on his father's side, his relatives were halwais (sweet-makers).
