The Shiv Sena has ended the suspense over who its candidates for Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) mayor and deputy mayor posts will be.The party declared Vishwanath Mahadeshwar and Hamangi Waralikar its candidates for the mayor and deputy mayor's posts respectively.This is third term of Mahadeshwar. 
In his first tenure, he was the chairman of BMC's Education Committee. 
Mahadeshwar also served as a member of the standing committee. 
Mahadeshwar, a well-educated man, served as the principal of Raje Sambhaji Junior college. 
In this election, Mahadeshwar won from ward number 87 by defeating BJP's Mahesh Parkar and Congress' Dharmesh Vyava.MAHADESHWAR, WORLIKAR FILE NOMINATIONOn Saturday, Mahadeshwar and Worlikar filed their nomination for mayor and deputy mayor's posts.
