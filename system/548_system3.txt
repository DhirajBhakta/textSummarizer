The only advice I have for youngsters is that concentrate more on your talent than six pack abs, says Chakraborty, who has been part of films such a Shaukeen (1982), Swami Dada (1982) and Disco Dancer (1982).The 67-year-old actor is back on the small screen as part of the comedy show, The Drama Company, in which he is working with big television comedians  such as Krushna Abhishek, Ali Asgar and Sugandha Mishra.
