An aircraft of the Chief Minister of Odisha Naveen Patnaik was pushed and pulled on the runway of an airport by the security personnel in the four wheeler style !!
In a peculiar situation, police personnel from Rourkela had to push Odisha CM Naveen Patnaik's aircraft as it underwent some technical difficulties.The incident occurred at Rourkela Airport, where the Chief Minister's flight stood idle on the runway for a while after landing.
