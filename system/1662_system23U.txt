Pakistan batsmen weren't aggressive enough while the bowlers looked jaded, contributing to a whitewash in the three-Test series against Australia, chief selector Inzamam-ul-Haq has said."
Our key batsmen like Younis Khan, Asad Shafiq and others just played a couple of good knocks in the three Test series and only Azhar Ali was consistent with more good knocks. 
Our main bowlers have been playing regularly in the last three series and the fatigue could have affected their performance," he added.Reliance on spinner Yasir Shah has been excessive, another factor in Pakistan's losses."
