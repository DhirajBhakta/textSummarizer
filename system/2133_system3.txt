Islamabad, Dec 8 (PTI) Pakistans electronic media watchdog today issued a notice to a TV channel for airing an unauthentic audio clip of crashed PIA PK-661 flight and asked it to explain by December 15 why action should not be taken against it. 
Soon after the crash, an audio clip went viral on social media claiming to be the last audio before the plane crashed and was broadcast by Channel 24. 
In the audio, people could be heard crying for help, however, it surfaced it was an audio from another crash, The Express Tribune reported. 
The audio was released by Channel 24 during its 9:47pm transmission last night, following which, Pakistan Electronic Media Regulatory Authority (PEMRA) sent a notice to Channel 24 for broadcasting the audio clip and the channel management is required to respond by December 15.
