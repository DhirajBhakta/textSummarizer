Islamabad, Dec 8 (PTI) Pakistans electronic media watchdog today issued a notice to a TV channel for airing an unauthentic audio clip of crashed PIA PK-661 flight and asked it to explain by December 15 why action should not be taken against it. 
In the audio, people could be heard crying for help, however, it surfaced it was an audio from another crash, The Express Tribune reported. 
The channel has been asked to prove that the recording was from the passenger cabin of the ill fated aircraft and provide evidence of how the audio was recorded and sent to the channel from the plane.
