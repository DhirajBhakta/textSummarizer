Dead body of a woman aged around 20 to 25 was found in a agricultural farm off the Palm Beach Road in Navi Mumbai on Monday afternoon. 
Mahendra Tandel, owner of the farm, was on a regular visit, when he found a suitcase dumped at one corner. 
While he tried lifting it, he found it be heavy. 
Feeling suspicious he alerted the police and a team from NRI coastal police station reached the spot.When the police officials opened the suitcase, they found a headless body of a woman. 
It is suspected that the woman was murdered and her body was stuffed inside the suitcase and dumped at the farm, which is located in a secluded area. 
The body has been sent for autopsy at the Navi Mumbai Municipal Hospital to ascertain the cause and time of her death.DCP S Pathare from Navi Mumbai said, "We have sent pictures of the deceased to all the police stations and control rooms to establish her identity. 
We are checking missing people's records as well".In 2012, decomposed remains of actress Sandhya Singh was found from a spot not far from this farmhouse. 
Sandhya's son Raghuveer was accused of her murder.
