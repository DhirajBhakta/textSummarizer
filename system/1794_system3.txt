The Union home ministry has cleared a proposal to add 15,000 more personnel to the Delhi Police and it awaits the finance ministrys nod, home minister Rajnath Singh said on Saturday terming its strength as inadequate.Singh made the announcement at a mega police event where ranks were awarded to 24 personnel out of the 27,000 promoted this year.The expansion, if effected, will make the force nearly one lakh strong.I feel Delhi Police strength is not adequate. 
I had told the police commissioner to promote personnel when it is due without any hesitation, Singh said.According to official data, Delhi Police at present has a total sanctioned strength of around 82,242, of which 77,083 are filled.
