Ludhiana, May 12 (PTI) A Ludhiana court has issued a fresh non-bailable warrant against actress Rakhi Sawant for allegedly making objectionable remarks against sage Valmiki, who wrote the Hindu epic Ramayana. 
The court of Judicial Magistrate Vishav Gupta yesterday fixed June 2 as the next date for hearing in the case. 
A local advocate Narinder Adiya had filed a complaint against Rakhi on July 9 last year alleging that she had hurt the religious sentiments of the Valmiki community by making derogatory comments against the sage.
