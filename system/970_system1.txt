Austen becomes the first female writer (following in the footsteps of William Shakespeare and Charles Dickens) to feature on a banknote.The note, launched by the Bank of England at Austens resting place, Winchester Cathedral, includes an image of the writer and one of her most beloved characters, Elizabeth Bennet. 
The author follows Florence Nightingale and Elizabeth Fry in featuring on notes.Tuesdays banknote launch is one of a series of events taking place to mark the 200th anniversary of Austens death, including the unveiling of a statue in Basingstoke, near her birthplace.
