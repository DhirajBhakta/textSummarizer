The fringe outfit claimed Padmavati had committed Jauhar (an act of killing self) to protect the Rajput honour and vehemently refuted that Allaudin Khilji had seen Padmavati's face through a mirror or there was any romantic liaison between the two . 
They claimed historical facts were distorted and characters of Padmavati and Khilji were portrayed in a manner which hurt Rajput pride.
