Vijayawada, Dec 21 (PTI) Demonetisation has led to a 30 per cent drop in revenues for Andhra Pradesh, Finance Minister Yanamala Ramakrishnudu said today but hoped implementation of Goods and Services Tax (GST) might help increase the Government income. 
Yanamala said the State registered an economic growth of 12.23 per cent in the first half of the current fiscal with agriculture and allied sectors contributing 24.44 per cent.
