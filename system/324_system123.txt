New Delhi, Jul 27 (PTI) North Delhi Mayor Preety Agarwal today directed the municipal commissioner for facilitating councillors with funds to undertake development works in their respective wards. 
The mayor gave the direction to commissioner P K Gupta during a meeting of the North Delhi Municipal Corporation (NDMC). 
"She directed that each councillor be provided with Rs 25 lakh for development work in his/her respective wards," an official of the civic body said.
