Out of nearly 1.05 million people who fled the fighting after the offensive against IS began last October, 825,000 remain displaced, the International Organization for Migration (IOM) said in a statement. 
But it is still near-impossible to access the Old City, where sweeping and demining operations continue.IOMs chief of mission in Iraq, Thomas Lothar Weiss, said the new displacement figures underscored the enormous crisis in the area.
