A 45-year-old man was allegedly attacked with cricket stumps in southeast Delhis Jaitpur area after he blamed the Prime Minister for long queues outside ATMs.The victim, Lallan Singh Kushwaha, received head injuries for making comments against demonetisation while on his way to buy a television from a shop in Ismailpur.Kushwaha saw long queues outside a nearby ATM where people were reportedly jostling against others in a bid to get cash.Kushwaha, a painter, allegedly blamed PM Modi for the chaos and said people across the nation are suffering due to note ban.
