Angelo Mathews has stepped down as Sri Lanka skipper across all three formats after his team lost the ODI series against Zimbabwe 2-3. 
This was Sri Lankas first-ever loss to Zimbabwe in a bilateral series and it continued their torrid run in 2017.Following Sri Lankas three-wicket loss to Zimbabwe in the final ODI at Hambantota which led to their series loss, Mathews had said that he was reconsidering his decision to be captain of Sri Lanka.
