A man allegedly severed two legs of a two-month-old puppy after the canine gave him a minor scratch in southwest Delhis Dwarka following which police has initiated action against the accused under the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act.The incident happened last week when the puppy entered Pramods house and the latter offered him food, police said.In what may be called a love or its eagerness, the dog scratched the accuseds leg with a paw.
