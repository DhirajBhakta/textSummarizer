Theatre actor Malavika Mohanan has bagged the lead role in Iranian auteur Majid Majidi's India-set film Beyond The Clouds.Daughter of renowned cinematographer KU Mohanan, Malavika was considered to be apt for the character in the tale of human relationships, read a statement.A spokesperson on behalf of Zee Studios and Eyecandy, said, "Malavika is on board for the film and Majidi has already begun shooting with her since a week in Mumbai."
