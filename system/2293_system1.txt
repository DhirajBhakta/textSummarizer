Once the road and lane marks are in place, they will help the traffic police in regulating the flow of traffic, Sandip Chandra, head of the Noida authoritys traffic cell, said. 
There are 112 traffic signals in Noida and the traffic police has 278 personnel at its disposal to regulate the movement of vehicles.The traffic police wants to make use of CCTVs to impose penalty on motorists violating traffic norms.
