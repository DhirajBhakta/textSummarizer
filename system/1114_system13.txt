						According to KK Dahiya, special transport commissioner, Approximately 20 acre land is being utilised in Bawana and Narela to develop new bus depots that will have multilevel parking.Besides urban specifications and safety tests, the buses are fitted with 41 anti-vandalism seats (that wont come off easily) and have a better seating arrangement to ensure the comfort of passengers.With the addition of 100 buses, the cluster fleet strength has risen to 1,725.
