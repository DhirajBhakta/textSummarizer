I heard some people hurled eggs. 
I am a non-vegetarian. 
If BJD and Congress workers hurl eggs at me, I'll make omelette with it and eat that."
These were the words of Minister of State for Heavy Industries Babul Supriyo after he was asked to respond to the incident of eggs being thrown at Union Minister Jual Oram on the latter's visit to Odisha's Kendrapara.On 13 June, Union Minister of Tribal Affairs, Jual Oram was on a visit to Aul Bazaar in Kendrapara district, where both Congress and BJD workers protested over his presence.
