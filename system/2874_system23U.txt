Terrorists today opened fire at a CRPF convoy near the Army cantonment in Srinagar's Pantha Chowk and fled towards a school nearby. 
Another soldier and a civilian have sustained injuries in the attack claimed by Lashkar-e-Taiba (LeT). 
The militants fled towards Delhi Public School (DPS) Srinagar, which has been cordoned off. 
The school was closed at the time of the attack.
