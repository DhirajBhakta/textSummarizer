The Karnataka government transferred on Monday deputy inspector general of prisons Roopa D Moudgil, who had recently alleged that AIADMK leader VK Sasikala received preferential treatment in Bengaluru Central Prison by paying a bribe. 
In a statement, the government clarified on Monday that the transfers were ordered to maintain the sanctity of the inquiry process and safeguard it from further interference.The state also criticised both Moudgil and Rao for speaking to the media.
