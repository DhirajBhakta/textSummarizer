Morning walkers on the beach of temple town Puri were in for a shock on Monday when they discovered the body of a dead whale that had been swept ashore by the waves.The 30-feet-long carcass was seen lying on the beach near the Pantha Niwas. 
Later, forest officials reached the spot to recover the carcass. 
The carcass resulted in a foul smell that spread across the area and a crane was brought in to lift the dead whale away.This is the second such incident in less than two months on the Puri beach.
