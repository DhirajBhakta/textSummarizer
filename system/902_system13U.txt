 The Maharashtra government has decided to increase compensation for rape and acid attack survivors to Rs10 lakh. 
Considering this, we have categorised compensation for different cases, said Vinita Singhal, principal secretary, women and child development department.In the most cruel crime, where the survivor faces permanent disability, financial assistance will be Rs10 lakh. 
Of this, 25% or Rs2.5 lakh will be disbursed immediately to the survivor while the remaning Rs7.5 lakh will be a fixed deposit in the bank and the survivor will continue to get monthly interest on it, she said.For other cases, the monetary help will be Rs3 lakh, Rs2 lakh and Rs1 lakhUnder the old scheme, the state government would give a maximum of Rs3 lakh to survivors who suffered from complete disfigurement of the face or physical handicap in case of an acid attack, while those with minor injuries were getting Rs50,000.Apart from the monetary compensation, the government, under this scheme, also provides counselling to the survivors and vocational or educational training.
