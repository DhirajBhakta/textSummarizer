New Delhi, Jul 24 (PTI) A new hostel is going to come up on the Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU) campus here, where 75 per cent of the rooms will be allotted to the students from the north-east, Union minister Jitendra Singh said today. 
To be built at an estimated cost of Rs 29 crore, 75 per cent of the rooms in the new hostel would be allotted to the students from the north-east, said Singh, the Union minister of state for development of north eastern region (DoNER).
