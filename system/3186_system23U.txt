Call it the impact of the infamous topper scam which last year exposed how students appearing in the Bihar Board became fake toppers by paying huge amount of money, 64 per cent of the Class 12 students of the Bihar Board have failed in this year's examinations, the results of which was declared today."
In the Arts stream, 533915 students gave the exams and 330338 failed (61.87 per cent).The results clearly shows that how the Bihar School Examination Board tightened the screw this year resulting in large number of students failing in the exams.
