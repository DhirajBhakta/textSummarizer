Shimla, Mar 26 (PTI) Veteran actor Anupam Kher, who grew up in Himachal Pradesh, today bought his first ever house here and gifted it to his mother. 
Kher said he always wanted to own a property in Shimla as he has spent all his childhood here. 
The actor shared the happy news on Twitter by sharing a video with fans and followers. 
"We Indians want to have our own home in a place where we grew up. 
Happy to share, we have our 1st ever home in Shimla. 
Presented it to Mom," Kher captioned the clip.
