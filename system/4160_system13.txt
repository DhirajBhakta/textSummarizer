Talking to India Today about the question raised by China on his Arunachal Pradesh visit, Dalai Lama said he goes there to spread the message of peace and co-existence and China can come and see what he preaches there. 
The spiritual leader also visited the Raman Reti Ashram, where a religious programme was organised.Commenting on the political changes in several countries recently, including the United States, the Dalai Lama said that change is the universal truth, but only the change that takes place in the hearts of the people.
