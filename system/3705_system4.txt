Two-time WWE Intercontinental champion Cody Rhodes is unable to use his surname for work outside the company, the 30-year-old said in an interview with si.com.The son of famed late Dusty Rhodes (Virgil Runnels) and younger brother of Goldust (Dustin Runnels), Cody said the WWE bars him from using his own surname for activites outside the company as they own intellectual property rights to the pro-wrestling character of Cody Rhodes.Cody has been wrestling for several other notable promotions ever since, including Impact Wrestling, Ring Of Honor (ROH), New Japan Pro Wrestling (NJPW), EVOLVE and PWG among others.
