Professor Yash Pal, eminent scientist and academician, died at the age of 90 at his Noida house late on Monday night. 
The science show was hugely popular among school children.Yash Pal studied Physics from Panjab University before earning a PhD degree in Physics from the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1958.He started his career with the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) as a member of the cosmic rays group.
