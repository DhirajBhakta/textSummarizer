Officials said that the camp was set up to ensure privacy to women pilgrims and ensure that no untoward incident takes place, as reported last year.?
Last year, a woman pilgrim was taking a bath and a man was scanning his mobile phone in the vicinity when the woman spotted him. 
said Atul Kumar, subdivisional magistrate, Modi Nagar.Since then, it was felt that a separate facility be set up for women pilgrims, officials said.?
The camp includes medical facilities and women constables are also deployed for security.The camp offers the best possible facilities to women pilgrims who walk afoot during the annual Kanwar pilgrimage from Hardiwar to Ghaziabad and beyond, officials said.?
