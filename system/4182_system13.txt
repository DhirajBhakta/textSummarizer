Pakistani Army today violated cease fire on Line of Control (LoC) in Balakote area of Poonch in Jammu.Sources said that Pakistani troops started medium machine gun fire on two Indian posts since 6.15 am. 
Two Pakistani posts, Peer Baba and Shalti were reportedly targeting two posts in forward areas on LoC. 
Sources said that Pakistan is giving cover fire to the terrorists so that they can infiltrate from this side.
