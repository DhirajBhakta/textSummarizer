Delhi deputy chief minister and Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) leader Manish Sisodia today said his Twitter account was hacked after anti-Hazare messages were retweeted from his account.He said somebody else was retweeting from his Twitter handle.One such anti-Hazare message retweeted from his account read: "No doubt he (Anna Hazare) has taken all of us for a ride: now I strongly feel that he is an agent of BJP".
