Hyderabad, Feb 26 (PTI) Adding another feather in her cap, star shuttler Saina Nehwal, who was appointed a member of the International Olympic Committees Athletes Commission (AC) last year, would be representing the panel in the Badminton World Federation (BWF). 
She would join the BWF Athletes Commission as a representative of the IOC AC, IOC said in a communication to Nehwal. 
The Chair of BWF AC has informed others on the panel about Sainas joining the BWF AC as a representative of the IOC AC.
