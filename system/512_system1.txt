In a freak accident in east Delhis Old Seelampur, a four-year-old girl died after she fell in a pot filled with hot tea.The girl fell into the freshly boiled beverage head first on Saturday, receiving severe burns to her face and upper body parts. 
The siblings who tried to help also burnt their limbs.The child, meanwhile, was immediately rushed to a nearby hospital where she underwent treatment for two days, but the girl could not survive.Our probe has not pointed to any foul play.
