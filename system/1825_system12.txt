Steve Smith today slammed his 17th hundred in only his 90th Test innings as Australia maintained their dominance over Pakistan in the rain-marred Boxing Day Test at the Melbourne Cricket Ground. 
(Scorecard)Skipper Smith cut Sohail for three runs past point to bring up his century from 168 balls in the 49th Test of his career. 
Interestingly, this was Smith's third consecutive Boxing Day Test hundred.
