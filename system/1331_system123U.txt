Presenting the Union Budget 2017 in Lok Sabha today, Finance Minister Arun Jaitley announced important changes in the Income Tax slab. 
Personal income tax in Rs 2.5 lakh - 5 lakh bracket has been reduced to 5 per cent from 10 per cent.Apart from lowering Income Tax rates, one of the major highlights of Jaitley's Budget proposal was new rules concerning political donation.
