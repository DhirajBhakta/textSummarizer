New Delhi, Jul 7 (PTI) Cement major Ultratechs Chairman and Non-Executive Director Kumar Mangalam Birla received an annual remuneration of Rs 22.50 crore in FY2016-17 in contrast to just Rs 3.3 lakh he earned from his groups telecom arm Idea Cellular. 
The ratio of Birlas remuneration to the median remuneration of employees was at 387.90, it added. 
The ratio of his remuneration in Idea Cellular to the median remuneration of employees was 0.42.
