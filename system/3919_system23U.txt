Ever since it rose to the pedestal it is at today, Coldplay has kept its legion of fans intact. 
But what the British band's frontman, Chris Martin, recently did for a fan is bound to swell up your heart even if you don't enjoy Coldplay's music.Reportedly, just before performing at a concert in Manila, Philippines, Martin went to meet Ken Valiant Santiago--a fan suffering from stage 4 cancer who couldn't attend the band's gig owing to his medical condition.
