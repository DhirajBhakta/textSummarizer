London, May 23 (PTI) The worlds largest aircraft has successfully completed a test flight, bringing the massive helium-filled airship one step closer to commercial use. 
On May 10, the Airlander 10 flew for a total of 180 minutes to test the aircrafts handling, improved landing technology and more, according to Hybrid Air Vehicles, the British company that built the aircraft. 
During the successful test flight in Cardington, UK, the Airlander 10 "handled superbly," said Dave Burns, the Airlander 10s chief test pilot.
