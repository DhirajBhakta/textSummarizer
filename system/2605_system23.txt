It didnt take much time for the terrorists to create the mayhem in south Kashmirs Anantnag on Monday night when seven pilgrims were killed and several others injured while returning from the Amarnath cave shrine.Survivors of the terrorist attack said had it not been for the driver of the bus they were travelling in, many more would have lost their lives.Recounting the attack, the survivors said they did not realise what was happening as many were sleeping and the gunmen fired indiscriminately in the dark.The firing did not last for more than 20 seconds.
