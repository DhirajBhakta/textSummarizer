A blast was reported in a Bhopal-Ujjain passenger train, numbered 59320, on Tuesday morning. 
The two seriously-injured people were referred to a hospital in Bhopal.The others were undergoing treatment at a hospital in Kalapipal town, which is close to the accident site - located around 81 km from the Madhya Pradesh capital.? Madhya Pradesh's IG Law and order Makrand Devaskar confirmed that the passenger train explosion was a terrorist attack.? Devaskar said that it was an IED blast and the police has arrested three persons from Pipariya.
