Rohan Bopanna and Pablo Cuevas yet again failed to click as a team, losing the closely-fought opening round of the ATP Indian Wells Masters to Novak Djokovic and Viktor Troicki, on Saturday.Bopanna and Cuevas lost 6-2 3-6 10-7 to the Serbian combination in one hour and six minutes. 
The winners logged 55 points, just two more than Bopanna and Cuevas.Bopanna and his partner from Uruguay were playing their third tournament together this season and have managed to win just one match at the Australian Open.
