The citys first floating hotel or floatel will be inaugurated by chief minister Devendra Fadnavis on Saturday evening.The vessel is harboured near the BWSL toll plazas since a few months now, and can be accessed via a jetty near the sea link500 meters before the toll plazas. 
It has a sky deck, two galleys, and will have two multi-cuisine restaurants including a club lounge equipped with a round-the-clock coffee shop.The floatel will give a 360-degree view of Mumbai and the Arabian Sea and has a capacity to cater to around 650 guests.
