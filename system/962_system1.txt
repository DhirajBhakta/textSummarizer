Soon after the voting started, BJP legislator OP Sharma claimed that at least 10-12 AAP legislators were likely to vote for the NDA candidate and it will become clear after the votes are counted on July 20. 
Replying to a specific question over the BJPs claims, Delhi chief minister Arvind Kejriwal said, We are supporting Meira Kumar (opposition candidate)  all should vote according to their conscience.While BJPs claims will be tested after the counting of votes, sacked AAP minister Kapil Mishra said he voted for the candidate who is all set to be the next president.
