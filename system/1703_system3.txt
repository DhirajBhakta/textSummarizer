Over 40 Indian sailors are stranded in UAE's Ajman anchorage and have sought help from External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj.Tweeting to Sushma Swaraj and Prime Minister Narendra Modi, the sailors and their families shared their plight and asked them to save their lives. 
Apparently, two out of the four ships are leaking and the passports of the sailors have been seized by the owner who remains untraceable, reported Indian Express.
