External Affairs Minister Sushma Swaraj has advised Geeta, the deaf and mute woman who was brought from Pakistan to India, not to leave her premises without informing anyone. 
Besides, she also asked the deaf and mute institute where Geeta is lodged to send the latter's activity report on a daily basis.Geeta had gone missing on July 6 for a brief period from Indore's Institute for Speech and Hearing Impaired.
