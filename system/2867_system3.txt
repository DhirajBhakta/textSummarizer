After the gruesome terror attack today that claimed the lives of two Central Reserve Police Force (CRPF) personnel, Additional Director General of Police (ADG) of CRPF S N Srivastava said that such 'cowardly act' does not affect the morale of security forces."
There is no security lapse," Srivastava told media.Earlier in the day, two CRPF jawans lost his life and two were injured after terrorists attacked their vehicle near Srinagar.According to Inspector General CRPF Ravideep Sahi the area has been cordoned off. 
Moments after the attack, terror outfit Lashkar-e-Taiba (LeT) said that it was behind the terror act.
