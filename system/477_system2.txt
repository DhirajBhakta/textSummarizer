 A cybercriminal posed as a bank official and cheated a 32-year-old woman from Versova of Rs49,999, said police.The man called the 35-year-old woman at 11.15 am on Sunday, saying the  bank needed to update its data following the introduction of the Goods and Services Tax (GST). 
The police are in the process of tracing the accuseds phone. 
Despite several reminders from the police and the media, people continue to fall prey to such scams. 
People should never share details of their debit or credit card with anyone. 
A genuine bank official will never call you and ask for your details, said a cyber expert.
