The handycams would be used by the in-charge of patrol vans to record the crime scene, gathering of people, and areas around the spot, apart from recording statements of the witnesses, all in digital format. 
The objective of the initiative is to get first-hand evidence of any crime and use it to make the case stronger against the accused, said a senior police officer.In the current scenario, the officer said, evidence collection is the investigating officers job and the officer takes the help of forensic experts to lift samples.
