The Yogi Adityanath government of Uttar Pradesh will not distribute the some 60 lakh ration cards printed by the Akhilesh Yadav government.These ration cards have photograph of the then Chief Minister Akhilesh Yadav. 
But, the Yogi Adityanath government has decided to roll back all the ration cards having photographs of Akhilesh Yadav.Akhilesh government had hastily got printed about 60 lakh ration cards ahead of Uttar Pradesh Assembly election.
