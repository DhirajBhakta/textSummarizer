Jail has made Sanjay Dutt a changed man, and as it turns out, a poet as well.While the Munna Bhai actor was in Yerwada jail, he penned a number of poems, one of which called O Meri Aangan Ki Chidiya Hai Tu will be used in his upcoming film Torbaaz.Sanjay Dutt is playing an army officer in Torbaaz, directed by Girish Malik. 
This is the first time an Indian film will deal with the theme of child soldiers.According to a report in Mumbai Mirror, the producer of Torbaaz got emotional when he heard the song O Meri Aangan Ki Chidiya Hai Tu at Sanjay Dutt's residence."
