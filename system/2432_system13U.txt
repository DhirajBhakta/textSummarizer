With his brilliant understanding of human emotions, Onir takes a leap into the forbidden love stories of the society with his latest venture.Shab traces the lives of four characters - their personal struggles and love lives. 
Even when showing stereotypical mindsets in our society, he makes it a point to balance out the narrow-minded comments by placing sane-minded characters nearby, giving them at least one or two jibes to throw at the prejudiced characters.
