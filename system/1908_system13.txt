From Anisur Rahman Dhaka, Dec 24 (PTI) A woman and a teenage boy today blew themselves up in the national capital when Bangladesh polices elite counter-terrorism unit raided their three-storey building where heavily-armed militants, belonging to an Islamist group blamed for the deadly cafe attack, were hiding. 
Earlier four persons, including two woman and as many children, came out of the building and surrendered to law enforcers, bdnews reported.
