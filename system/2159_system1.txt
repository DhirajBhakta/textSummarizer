Retired school teachers will now be appointed in Delhi government-run schools in case of shortage of teachers or temporary vacancies.Deputy chief minister and education minister Manish Sisodia on Tuesday said school principals can appoint retired teachers without getting prior approval from the education department.?
If there is shortage of teachers in any school, then the principal need not ask the deputy director or send the file to him. 
Sisodia said.He said the decision has been taken to ensure children do not suffer due to lack of teachers or in case existing teachers go on leave or training.Sisodia announced the decision during the annual function of a Sarvodaya Vidyalaya in Mayur Vihar Phase I.?
