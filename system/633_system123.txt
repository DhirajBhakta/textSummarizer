 Farmers outfits in Maharashtra will restart their protest to demand a blanked loan waiver and implementation of the Swaminathan commissions recommendations across the state by staging rasta roko on August 14. 
The awareness march (Janjagruti Yatra) was started from July 10 to tell people about the alleged injustice being done to farmers.We have decided to take our agitation forward by holding rasta roko on August 14.
