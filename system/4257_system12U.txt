Los Angeles, Mar 14 (PTI) Away from home, Priyanka Chopra played Holi, the festival of colours, with popular talk show host Jimmy Fallon. 
The 34-year-old actress, who is currently shooting for the TV series "Quantico" in the US, indulged in an impromptu celebration on "The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon". 
Priyanka took to Twitter to share her colourful experience by writing, "This was so fun @jimmyfallon my favourite part besides your shocked face after I got you with a big blob of face paint was @theroots playing #RangBarse."
