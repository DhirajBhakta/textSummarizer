New Delhi, Jul 17 (PTI) PremjiInvest, the investment arm of Wipro Chairman Azim Premji, today bought 3.26 per cent stake in Kishore Biyanis Future Lifestyle Fashions Ltd (FLFL) for nearly Rs 196 crore through open market transactions. 
As per the block deal data available with NSE, PI Opportunities Fund 1, part of PremjiInvest, today purchased 62 lakh shares, or 3.26 per cent stake, in FLFL.
