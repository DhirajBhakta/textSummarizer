The reverberations of the Darjeeling unrest are being felt in the National Capital too with Gorkhas today protesting against Mamata Banerjee and the West Bengal government at Jantar Mantar.Hundreds of Gorkhas in Delhi gathered at Jantar Mantar with national flags and banners saying 'We want Gorkhaland'.In Darjeeling, the Gorkha Janmukti Morcha's (GJM) indefinite strike entered its eighth day today. 
Bimal Gurung-led GJM had alleged that some of its partymen were killed in clashes with the police.
