Bihar Chief Minister Nitish Kumar has ruled himself out of the PM race in 2019. 
Speaking to reporters on the sideline of an event in Patna, Nitish said he has no interest in taking the top post."
I was elected as party (JD-U) president as Sharadji (Yadav) couldn't continue on the post. 
But this was seen as my aspiration for a national role...which is wrong," he said.
