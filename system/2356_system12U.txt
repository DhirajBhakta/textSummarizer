Officials of UP Jal Nigam said that the Upper Ganga Canal (UGC) has been temporarily shut due to silt deposit caused by heavy rains in Uttarakhand.The UGC, which originates from Haridwar, supplies Ganga water to two treatment plants at Pratap Vihar in Ghaziabad where the water is treated and supplied to trans-Hindon areas of Ghaziabad and neighbouring Noida.The water supply through the canal has been stopped.
