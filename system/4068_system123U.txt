In the 30th edition of Mann Ki Baat, Prime Minister Narendra Modi addressed the nation for the first time after BJP's resounding success in Assembly elections 2017.Modi in his national radio address 'Mann Ki Baat' wished prosperity for the neighbouring country, recalling the common memories of Rabindranath Tagore shared by both India and Bangladesh."
We all are proud that both the nations share the memories of Gurudev Rabindra Nath Tagore who wrote the national anthem of both India and Bangladesh," the Prime Minister said.
