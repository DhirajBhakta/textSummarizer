A 16-year-old Class 10 student allegedly raped by an auto driver for eight months gave birth to a premature girl at her schools washroom in northwest Delhis Mukherjee Nagar on Thursday. 
The authorities rushed the girl and her premature baby to a nearby hospital for medical attention.Milind Dumbre, deputy commissioner of police (northwest), said the condition of the girl and her daughter was out of danger and stable.
