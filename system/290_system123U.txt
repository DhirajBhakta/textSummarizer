A 59-year old woman from the central Chinese city of Wuhan transformed her appearance through plastic surgery in order to avoid 25 million yuan ($3.71 million) of personal debts, state news agency Xinhua said. 
In a case highlighting the challenges facing China as it tries to establish a credit society, police officers were reported to be astonished after apprehending the woman, who fled to the southeastern Chinese city of Shenzhen after a court in Wuhan ordered her to pay off her debt.We were very surprised at the scene, the official Xinhua news agency quoted a policeman as saying.
