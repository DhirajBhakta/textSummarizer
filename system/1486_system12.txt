A seven-year-old boy died after drowning in an open drain in south Delhis Malviya Nagar on Thursday eveningRaja Babu was reportedly playing with his friends inside a park around 2 pm when the incident occurred. 
Babus friends told the police that he got stuck in the sludge and could not climb up. 
He was taken to the hospital and brought dead on arrival, a police officer said.
