Ten days after it began in inquiry into allegations of molestation against The Viral Fever CEO Arunabh Kumar, the Mumabi Police is likely to close the case, sources tell India Today. 
An advocate Rizwan Siddhiqui, had written to the force three times, urging it to file an FIR against Kumar, charging him with molestation.However, Mumbai Police sources tell India Today that the probe will be closed by this week without an FIR being filed as no victims have come forward. 
The sources added that no other girls who made similar allegations on social media have come forward.A top Mumbai Police officer said that Kumar will not be called to the police station to record a statement.
