Yadav was charged in connection to the scam in 2012, the same year he cleared his PMT (Pre-Medical Test). 
The scam involved 13 different exams conducted by Vyapam, for selection of medical students and state government employees (including food inspectors, transport constables, police personnel, school teachers, dairy supply officers and forest guards). 
The exams were taken by around 3.2 million students.
