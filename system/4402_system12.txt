Gujarat Home Minister Pradipsinh Jadeja today joined the long list of leaders, ministers facing a shoe attack when he was targeted outside the legislative Assembly. 
A man identified as Gopal Italia hurled a shoe at the minister when he was addressing reporters outside the Assembly.
