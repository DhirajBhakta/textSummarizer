A 15-year-old girl was repeatedly being gang raped by four men, including a local builder, at Jamia Nagar area of Delhi, city police said on Monday.Police said the assault on the girl first took place in July 2016 when the accused, identified as 40-year-old Sajid Khan, a resident of Shaheen Bagh area, raped the victim in his flat.The victim is a Class 9 student in a government school.
