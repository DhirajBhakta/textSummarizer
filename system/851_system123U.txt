A nine-year-old girl was fatally hit by an Army vehicle at Aglar village in Shopian district, South Kashmir, on Thursday. 
Police have registered an FIR against the driver, a member of the 55 Rashtriya Rifles.Arooba Farooq  a Class 2 student  was rushed to the sub-district hospital in Zainapora, where she succumbed to her injuries. 
Arooba was heading to school when she stopped to buy snacks for herself.
