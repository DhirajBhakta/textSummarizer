New Delhi, Feb 13 (PTI) Shares of Ruchi Soya Industries today surged over 17 per cent after the company signed an agreement with Patanjali Ayurved for physical refining and packaging of edible oils. 
The scrip skyrocketed 16.34 per cent to settle at Rs 29.90 on BSE. 
During the day, it zoomed 19.84 per cent to Rs 30.80. 
At NSE, shares of the company jumped 17.28 per cent to end at Rs 30.20.
