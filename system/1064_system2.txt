So you thought Ranbir Kapoor was just a barfi You doubted if he could get into the shoes of Bollywood's eternal bad boy Sanjay DuttWell, nay-sayers, here is the first look of Ranbir Kapoor from the sets of Dutt, a biopic that is being shot on the life of Sanjay Dutt, currently, by director Rajkumar Hirani.Tentatively titled Dutt, the film will focus on Sanjay's formative years, his relation with his father Sunil Dutt, his struggles coping up with the loss of his mother, his many affairs, etc.Paresh Rawal is playing the role of Sunil Dutt while Manisha Koirala, Sanjay's co-star in many film, is starring as Nargis, Sanju baba's mom.
