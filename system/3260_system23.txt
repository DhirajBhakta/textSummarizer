New Delhi, May 22 (PTI) The monthly subscriber report released by telecom regulator Trai for March shows that Reliance Jios low price 4G mobile service scheme Prime is not driving demand for its connections, while incumbents have gained, UBS Securities Asia said. 
"As per the March subscriber data released by TRAI, Jio reported net adds of 5.8 million versus 12.2 million in February," the report said.
