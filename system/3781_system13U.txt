Ministry New Delhi, Apr 16 (PTI) Over 1 crore households in urban areas of the country dont have bathrooms, according to data provided by the Ministry of Housing and Urban Poverty Alleviation (HUPA). 
So far, 31.14 lakh individual toilets have been constructed under the programme, while 1.15 lakh community and public toilets have been constructed with the assistance of the government.
