Harshvardhan Kapoor, who made his big Bollywood debut with Mirzya, went onto win a handful of Best Debut trophies at various award functions. 
And the 26-year-old actor was expecting the same at Filmfare Awards which took place recently. 
But to his surprise, the Best Debut trophy went to Diljit Dosanjh for Udta Punjab. 
And this hasn't gone down too well with Junior Kapoor.
