24 Kawariyas, 6 ITBP and Police personnel were injured in stone pelting by a mob during the 'Kanwar Yatra' in Bareilly. 
The police had to resort to a lathi charge to disperse the crowd.Gajendra Kumar, Senior Superintendent of Police said, 'We are making arrests and one police officer has been suspended; (the) situation (is) peaceful at the moment'.R. 
Vikram Singh, District Magistrate, Bareilly, said the Kanwariyas "did not have permission" to pass through the route, which fell under pockets of Muslim population.
