Bandras Rizvi Education Society, which manages seven institutes including schools and colleges, recently implemented the first phase of digital transformation of its education and administration system. 
The education society also plans to introduce online assessment of all examinations soon.Paperless admissions have not only reduced the work of our staff but have also made the process very transparent for everybody. 
The society spent around Rs1 crore and took seven months to implement the first phase.He added that in the second phase, the education society plans to start online assessment of exam papers and make study material available online for all its 6,000 school and college students.The management trained its teachers in collaboration with a technology provider to ensure the final implementation is foolproof.
