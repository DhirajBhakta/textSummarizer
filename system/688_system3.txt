Indu Sarkar, a film on Emergency, will hurt sentiments of many Congressmen and that is what the present Prime Minister wants, veteran Congress leader M Veerappa Moily said on Sunday.Indu Sarkar, based on the 1975-77 Emergency period, has evoked strong criticism and protest from the Congress.The Congresss apprehensions are over the portrayal of party veterans like Indira Gandhi, Sanjay Gandhi and other senior leaders in the film.Filmmaker Madhur Bhandarkar, however, had said he would include a disclaimer in Indu Sarkar, stating that the film is mostly fictional.Talking to PTI, Moily said the film will hurt sentiments of Congressmen.It hurts the sentiments of many of the Congressmen and that is what the present Prime Minister wants.
