Steve Whitmire, the puppeteer who was fired after 27 years as Kermit the Frog has said he is devastated to have failed Muppets founder and his mentor Jim Henson.Whitmire has been with the Muppets since 1978, and took over as Kermit after Henson died in 1990. 
I feel that I am at the top of my game, and I want all of you who love the Muppets to know that I would never consider abandoning Kermit.For me the Muppets ... are a calling, an urgent, undeniable, impossible to resist way of lifeWhitmire said he was informed that Kermit would be recast in October, and has kept silent in the ensuing months in the hopes that he could get his bosses to change their minds.
