Crime marginally reduced this year in Delhi compared to the last year with only motor vehicle theft registering an increase over the previous year. 
Of these, police managed to solve only 1,744 cases.The total cases under the Indian Penal Code (IPC)  FIRs registered across Delhi  increased by 8.88% than last years 1.81 lakh to 1.97 lakh this year.
