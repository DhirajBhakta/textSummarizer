From K J M Varma Beijing, Mar 31 (PTI) China will launch a network of 156 mini-satellites to facilitate global broadband coverage as part of the countrys effort to give a wider push for commercial space development. 
This is the first low orbiting, networked satellite project, which will orbit 1,000 km above the ground, state-owned China Aerospace Science and Industry Corporation (CASIC) said.
