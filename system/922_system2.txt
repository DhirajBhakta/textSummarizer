However, for this purpose, they would need an additional 2,800 cameras on both the suburban railway sections.A proposal has been prepared by WR and CR officials which has been sent to the ministry of railways for approval. 
The proposal includes setting up the network of CCTV cameras in the station premises to monitor cleanliness at each station. 
As of now, pilot projects have been undertaken at Churchgate and Mumbai Central stations, said the officials.
