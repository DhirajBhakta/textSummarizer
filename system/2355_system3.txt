Four men died after inhaling toxic gases while cleaning a septic tank in south Delhi on Saturday morning. 
The incident was reported at around 10.43am, when police control room received information that the five men  cleaning the tank had not come out for a long time. 
The men had gone down to clean a tank at 100 feet road in Ghitorni. 
They were pulled out in an unconscious state by fire department personnel after an hour-long operation.Police said that five men were rushed to Fortis, AIIMS Trauma Centre and Safdarjung hospital. 
One of the men, Jaspal, who survived the incident is receiving treatment at a city hospital.
