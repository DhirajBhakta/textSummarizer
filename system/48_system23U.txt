Bollywood star Amitabh Bachchan, who is currently shooting for his upcoming film 102 Not Out, says sleeping on a films set is a gifted moment in the script for an actor.Sleep -- that mechanism that has been a constant in us all... like taking in air to breathe and survive... 
And the opportunity of catching up with its loss, on set is a gifted moment in the script, rare but so welcome, Amitabh wrote on his blog on Monday night.
