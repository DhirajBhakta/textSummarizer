Mumbai Customs arrested one Saudi Arabian Airlines employee for smuggling gold worth Rs 63.7 lakh at airport on Thursday night.As per the information shared to India Today, the Air Intelligence Unit (AIU) of Mumbai Customs intercepted one Saudi Arabian national named Goya Ahmad Siraj A. 
He had arrived from Jeddah by Saudi Arabian Airlines flight.During examination, the officials recovered two gold bars of 1000 grams each and one gold bar of 100 grams, totally weighing 2100 grams valued at Rs 63,71,400.
