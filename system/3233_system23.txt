Famous actor and BJP lawmaker Paresh Rawal alleged today that he was "coerced by Twitter" into removing a tweet targeting author Arundhati Roy. 
Paresh Rawal's tweet targeting Arundhati Roy created a lot of ruckus.Paresh Rawal referred to the controversial April 9 incident in Kashmir where Farooq Dar, a civilian was tied to the front of an army vehicle and paraded across at least 9 villages as a 'human shield' against stone pelters.
