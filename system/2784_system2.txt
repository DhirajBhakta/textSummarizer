FARMERS SUICIDE EVERYWHEREIn Hoshangabad, four farmers have committed suicide while three each from Badwani and Dhar districts took the extreme step.Two farmers each from Ratlam, Vidisha, Balaghat, Chhattarpur, and Panna also killed themselves, supposedly, because of debt burden and farm related issues while one farmer each from Sagar, Raisen, Harda, Shivpuri, Chindwara, Neemuch, Indore, Tikamgarh, Khandwa Dewas, Morena, Mandsaur and Narsinghpur committed suicide.
