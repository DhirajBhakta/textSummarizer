Within 18 hours of resigning as Bihar chief minister, Nitish Kumar took oath on Thursday as the head of the government for the sixth time with the BJPs Sushil Kumar Modi as his deputy.He will seek a trust vote on Friday after re-forging a partnership with the BJP, four years after he walked out of the NDA.His surprise resignation sank the three-party grand alliance of his Janata Dal (United), Lalu Prasads Rashtriya Janata Dal and Congress that defeated the BJP in the 2015 state elections.Kumars switch upset several leaders of the JD(U) as well, including parliamentarians and local unit heads.
