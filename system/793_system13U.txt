Financial year in India could soon be the same as calendar year as the Narendra Modi government is considering changing the fiscal year to January-December to coincide with the calendar year."
The matter of changing financial year is under consideration of the government," Finance Minister Arun Jaitley on Friday said in a written reply in the Lok Sabha.He was asked whether the government proposes to synchronise the financial year with the calendar year from January.The matter has been examined recently by a committee constituted by the government under the chairmanship of former Chief Economic Adviser Shankar Acharya.
