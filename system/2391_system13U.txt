Kriti Sanon is all set for her debut performance at the 18th IIFA awards and says she is extremely excited to perform with her Raabta co-star Sushant Singh Rajput.Sharing her excitement with ANI post the IIFA Press Conference, the 26-year-old said, I have never done a couple act before. 
After doing Raabta and another music video together, I share great comfort and chemistry with Sushant.Talking about the preparations for the performance before the main event, the Dilwale star said, We have rehearsed separately as well as together.
