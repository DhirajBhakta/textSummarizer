By Yoshita Singh and Lalit K Jha Washington, Jun 26 (PTI) Highlighting his achievements in rooting out corruption, Prime Minister Narendra Modi said that there has been "not even one taint or blot" on his government in the past three years. 
Modi said corruption was the main reason why governments in India were voted out in the past. 
"We are working to convert the aspiration of the people of India," he said amidst applause from the audience.
