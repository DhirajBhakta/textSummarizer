The poor fuel efficiency is due to racketeers using an electronic chip to dispense less fuel than what you were being charged for.The Thane crime branch on Saturday busted a state wide petrol pump racket wherein customers were being duped by petrol pump owners who used of an electronic chip that dispensed less fuel than the purchased quantity. 
A total of eleven petrol pumps have been sealed following arrests of several people involved in the racket including petrol pump owners and employees and engineers who developed the electronic chip.
