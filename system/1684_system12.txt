Ruling Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP), opposition Congress and new entity Aam Aadmi party (AAP) makes it triangular battle in Goa.Weeks before the poll AAP Chief and Delhi Chief Minister Arvind kejriwal kick starts his poll campaign in Goa today. 
Speaking in Goa's Benaulim assembly constituency, he invokes his anti corruption and anti communal stand saying he won't tolerate corruption or communal in his party.Kejriwal said, that any of his candidate found guilty of these charges, he won't hesitate to cancel their candidature even 24 hours ahead of the poll no matter seat is vacated.
