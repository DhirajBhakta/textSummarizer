By Neelabh Srivastava New Delhi, Jun 14 (PTI) Counterfeit currency detection instances in the countrys banking channels have seen an all time increase in the last eight years at over 3.53 lakh instances, according to a latest government report. 
"The number of counterfeit currency reports (CCRs) increased from a mere 8,580 in 2007-08 to 35,730 in 2008-09 and 3,53,837 in 2014-15," the report, accessed by PTI, said.
