Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister Chandrababu Naidu today said he will build a "bigger, better city" in Amaravati than Hyderabad when the latter becomes the capital of Telangana in 2024.
?"I will build a bigger, better city than Hyderabad. 
Amaravati, the Andhra capital, will be the best city in India," Naidu told Consulting Editor Rajdeep Sardesai at the India Today South Conclave in Chennai.
