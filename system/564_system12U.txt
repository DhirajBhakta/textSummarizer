Bollywood actor Sunny Deol refuted the reports that his son Karan was set to be launched by Yash Raj Films.There were reports that Karan was gearing up to be launched by Yash Raj Films but it could not happen when Aditya Chopra asked the young actor to sign a three-film deal, which Sunny objected to. 
Bobby Deol, Shreyas and Dharmendra were also present at the launch.Asked about choosing Sunny to play one of the character, Shreyas told media on Monday: I wanted Sunny sir to play the role because it adds credibility to the film.
