 From iron scraps to discarded sheets, broken pipes and tiles -  Delhi Metro has used all kinds of waste to develop a park in east Delhi?
						Delhi generates 10,000 tonnes of municipal solid waste, 30 tonnes electronic waste, 70 tonnes biomedical waste, 4,000 tonnes construction/demolition waste and 800 tonnes plastic waste daily. 
Green waste like leaves and grass will be recycled for making manure,? 
A carpentry shop has been set up for making use of packing wood and dismantled steel pipes. 
Broken tiles are being used for waterproofing of rooftops and new barricades are being made from broken pipes of used railings.
