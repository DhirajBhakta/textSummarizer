 An 18-month-old girl was killed while crossing the road with her mother after a car knocked her down in Santacruz (West) on Saturday. 
The driver has been arrested and booked for causing death due to negligence.According to the Santacruz police, the incident took place on the southbound stretch of Link Road near Ramkrishna Mission Hospital at around 5pm. 
The girl, Pooja Pawar, was walking next to her mother Sonabai Pawar when the car moving from Santacruz towards Khar hit her.The girl suffered head injuries and was taken to Bhabha Hospital but was declared dead on admission.
