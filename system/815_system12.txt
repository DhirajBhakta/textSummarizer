Nisheeth Jha, the husband of Assamese actor Bidisha Bezbaruah who allegedly committed suicide by hanging herself from a ceiling fan, was on Thursday sent to 14 days in judicial custody. 
Before a magistrate remanded him in judicial custody, Jha admitted to his interrogators that there was another woman in his life, police said.During a day-long police remand, Jha confessed that this strained his relations with Bidisha.
