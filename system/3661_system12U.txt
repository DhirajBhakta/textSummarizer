TV actor Parth Samthaan was on Sunday booked under Section 8 and 12 of POSCO Act in Bangur Nagar on molestation complaint filed by a model last month. 
The model had accused Samthaan of molestation and registered a complaint at the Bangar Nagar Police Station.The victim said she was 16 when she was molested by the actor. 
Samthaan has moved an anticipatory bail application in Dindoshi court, which is likely to be heard tomorrow.Samthaan was earlier in news after he accused former friend and TV producer Vikas Gupta of molestation.
