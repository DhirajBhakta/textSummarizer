Bihar Deputy Chief Minister Tejaswi Yadav today launched a scathing attack on Prime Minister Narendra Modi urging him to speak the truth on the occasion of Ram Navami. 
Respected PM, at least on the auspicious occasion of Ram Navami today, please speak the truth on when implementation will start on your poll promise of providing jobs to 2 crore youths every year" Tejaswi asked.Tejashwi's remark came in response to Prime Minister Narendra Modi's tweet greeting people on Ram Navami.
