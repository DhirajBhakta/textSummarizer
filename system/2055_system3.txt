Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal said today that the nation wants to know the educational qualifications of Prime Minister Narendra Modi in the light of demonetisation.Speaking to mediapersons, Kejriwal questioned Modi's educational qualifications and said the latter was not well-educated. 
However, the ruling BJP has outright rejected its demand saying it was not possible in any circumstances.The Delhi CM has raised the issue of Modi's educational qualification after a gap of several months.
