Taapsee Pannu, who will be seen alongside superstar Salman Khan in a song in Judwaa 2, says it is important to work with top actors as they have higher market value. 
The second instalment of 1997 hit film Judwaa has cameo of Salman and he recently shot for a song with Varun Dhawan, Jacqueline Fernandez and Taapsee. 
It may sound wrong but we have grown up watching Salman so we have this wow image about him, she added.The Pink actor said she now wants to do a full fledged film with Salman.
