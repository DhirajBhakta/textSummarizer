A rare 1931 pencil portrait of Mahatma Gandhi and a collection of his handwritten letters that went under the hammer at the Sothebys auction here on Tuesday have been bought by noted Indian businessman Cyrus Poonawalla, popularly known as the vaccine billionaire.Poonawalla founded the Serum Institute of India in 1966, today one of the worlds largest vaccine makers by volume, producing 1.3 billion doses annually.
