WILL UPROOT PAK-SPONSORED TERROR' This is the second time in two days that the home minister has directly spoken about Pakistan-backed terrorism in Kashmir. 
During a press conference yesterday, he had said, "I assure you that we will uproot Pakistan-sponsored terror from Jammu and Kashmir and establish peace there". 
Rajnath's comments today came two days after two Indian Army jawans were killed in an ambush my militants on the Jammu-Srinagar National Highway.
