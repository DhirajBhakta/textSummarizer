Mumbai, Apr 28 (PTI) Foreign exchange reserves were just short of the life-time high after rising by USD 1.250 billion to USD 371.14 billion in the week to April 21, helped by increase in foreign currency assets, the Reserve Bank said. 
The reserves had touched a life-time high of USD 371.99 billion in the week to September 30, 2016. 
In the previous week, reserves had risen by USD 889.4 million to USD 369.887 billion.
