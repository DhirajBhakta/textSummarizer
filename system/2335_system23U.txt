Actor Pankaj Tripathi  has been a part of popular films such as Agneepath (2012), Gangs of Wasseypur I and II (2012) and Fukrey (2013). 
Pankaj explains, I have been in the industry for many years now, but people still come up to me and say Sir, youre such a good actor, we loved watching you in that movie. 
The very fact that people make an effort to search and know more about me is a testament that I am doing my job well, he says.Pankaj is now gearing up to act alongside actor Rajinikanth in the upcoming multilingual film titled Kaala Karikalan, where he will play the role of a policeman.
