Melbourne Commonwealth Games gold medalist boxer Akhil Kumar and Olympian Jitender Kumar today confirmed turning professional after signing a multi-year deal with IOS Boxing, the company which has been managing Vijender Singh's pro-boxing career. 
(Latest Sports stories)Both the boxers are expected to make their pro debut when Vijender will take on China's WBO Oriental Super Middleweight champion Zulpikar Maimaitiali in Mumbai on April 1.Akhil and Jitender were in talks with the promoters for a long time and were even scheduled to make their pro boxing debut when Vijender beat Francis Cheka in December last year.
