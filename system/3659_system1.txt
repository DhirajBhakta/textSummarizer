A Kashmiri project fellow at BITS Pilani in Rajasthan has left the institute after allegedly facing harassment. 
Hashim Sofi, a 27-year-old scholar who was at BITS as a research project staff, has left for his hometown, Bandipora in Kashmir. 
Sofi had on April 20 reported to the chief warden of his hostel that someone had written objectionable comments on the door of his hostel room. 
As per Sofi, this incident happened on the intervening night of April 20 and 21. 
As per a release issued by BITS, Pilani, which was accessed by India Today, "The Chief Warden met and assured all assistance and help to the concerned project fellow.
