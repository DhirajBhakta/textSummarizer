Meet Meow-Ludo Disco Gamma Meow-Meow--yes, that's what this Australian man's legal name is. 
It's his unique implementation of science that has blown our minds.This man will no longer have to worry about losing his travel card. 
The implant, which, according to Meow, gives him 'an ability not everyone else has', is that of his Opal card chip, cut down to 10mmX6mm to suit the purpose.
