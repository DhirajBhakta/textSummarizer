Sanoj Yadav's Rashtriya Janata Dal (RJD) comradeship of 30 years failed to wane Bihar Health Minister Tej Pratap's ire towards him who thrashed him brutally in a party on Friday. 
An RJD loyalist and worker, Sanoj had a few days back appeared in a news debate on television where he was unable to defend Lalu Yadav and his family over allegations of corruption and acquiring benami properties.
