Addressing Kulbhushan Jadhav as an Indian RAW agent, Pakistan's Inter-Services Public Relations (ISPR) released a statement saying Kulbhushan Sudhir Jadhav, who was living as Hussein Mubarak Patel, was arrested on March 3 last year through a "Counter Intelligence Operation" from Balochistan's Mashkel. 
Kulbhushan Jadhav is an Indian citizen, recognised by the Indian government as a former naval officer, and was arrested by Pakistan alleging espionage. 
KULBHUSHAN JADHAV'S BACKGROUND Kulbhushan Jadhav joined the Indian National Defence Academy in the year 1987. 
In a video "evidence" Pakistan produced to prove that Kulbhushan confessed he is indeed a RAW agent, he could be heard saying he served in the Indian Navy till the 2001 Parliament attack happened.After the Parliament attack of 2001, Kulbhushan Jadhav went undercover as Hussain Mubarak Patel to gather information and intelligence within India.
