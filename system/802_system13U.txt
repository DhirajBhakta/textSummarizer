Punjab director general of police (DGP) Suresh Arora said on Thursday that the police will increase presence on social media, which is widely used by radicals to spread anger by spreading misinformation. 
This will be a proactive approach to tackle the hate spread online, he said while speaking to the media in Patiala.He said that some forces sitting in foreign countries have nefarious design to disturb peace and harmony in Punjab.
