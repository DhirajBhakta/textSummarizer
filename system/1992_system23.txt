Panaji, Dec 19 (PTI) The Aam Aadmi Party today declared Elvis Gomes, a former bureaucrat, as its chief ministerial candidate. 
The announcement was made at a rally?at Cuncolim in South Goa by the AAP national convenor and Delhi Chief Minister Arvind Kejriwal. 
Currently, the elected President of Goa Football Association, Gomes was IG Prisons and Urban Development Secretary when he quit service.
