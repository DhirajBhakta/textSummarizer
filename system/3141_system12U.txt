A beautician had visited her for makeup and Julie was sparkling in her bridal attire.Her house was all decked up, the pandit (priest) was there to chant sacred mantra and Abhinay Kumar, a 22-year-old youth she was to tie the nuptial knot, was ready, though under duress. 
But as usual, the cops arrived late, and by the time the marriage was already over.They still wanted to rescue the groom but the bride and her family - backed with relatives and villagers - put up a tough fight, forcing the cops to beat a hasty retreat.
