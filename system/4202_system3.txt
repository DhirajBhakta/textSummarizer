From Seema Hakhu Kachru Houston, Mar 17 (PTI) An American business group will organise a conference here to discuss the latest developments and best practices of doing business in India. 
The two-day Houston India conference conference will begin from March 24. 
The theme of the Conference is Make in India ? 
The Inside Story. 
India is one of the major business partners for companies in Texas. 
There is a large Indian presence in the educational, medical and research institutes that Houston is renowned for, they said. 
Texas is home to 54 (or 11 per cent) of Fortune 500 companies, the organisers said.
