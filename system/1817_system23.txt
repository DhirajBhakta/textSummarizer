New Delhi, Dec 29 (PTI) RBIs newly appointed Deputy Governor Viral Acharya, a noted economist, today said he will give his best in the new role. 
"I am honoured at being offered the opportunity and hope to give it my best," Acharya told PTI a day after being named the Deputy Governor. 
Acharya, who has been appointed for a term of three years, would take charge on January 20, the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) said in a late evening statement on Wednesday.
