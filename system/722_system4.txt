After Brihanmumbai Municipal Corporation (BMC) sent around 4,000 notices to housing societies, hotels, hospitals and other institutions in Andheri (East) for failing to segregate waste, it is holding a three-day exhibition to raise awareness among residents and school students about waste management. 
The ward office of the area is hosting more than 40 waste management companies from across the country to interact with residents. 
Citizens will be shown methods of segregating organic waste, processing it and vermi-composting it.?
