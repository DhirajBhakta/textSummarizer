The workers at a mobile phone factory in Greater Noida turned violent on Tuesday evening when the companys management sacked over 60 workers without any prior notice. 
The Chinese handset maker has been one of the official sponsors of the 10th edition of IPL this year.The growing resentment among the workers took a violent turn on Tuesday, when one of the guards of the company allegedly slapped a worker after he asked about his job.Soon after the incident, thousands of workers gathered and staged a protest against the errant policies of the company.
