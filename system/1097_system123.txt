Rajkummar Rao and Anjali Patil-starrer political satire Newton, which had its world premiere at the 67th Berlin International Film Festival (also called Berlinale), has won the International Federation of Art Cinemas (CICAE) award in its Forum segment.Rao shared the news on Twitter by congratulating the entire team of the Amit Masurkar-directed movie. 
Congratulations team @ManMundra@Amit_MasurkarThe movie, which also stars Pankaj Tripathi, revolves around the story of a rookie clerk on election duty in a conflict-ridden jungle of Central India, who tries his best to conduct free and fair voting.
