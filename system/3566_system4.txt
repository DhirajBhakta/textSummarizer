Now, Raj Thackeray-led Maharashtra Navnirman Sena (MNS) has written letters to building constructors and asked them to stop discrimination while selling flats in their buildings. 
We had raise this issue in BMC and had asked mayor to make a strict law so that there will not be any discrimination about not selling flats to non-vegetarian people. 
Now, we have intensified our agitation, and in extreme case, we may take law in our hands if situation remains the same," said Sandeep Deshpande, MNS spokesperson.MNS workers have been asked to gather information about such builders who don't sell flats to non-vegetarian people.
