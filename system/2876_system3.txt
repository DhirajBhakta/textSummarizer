The minister of state also said, "With a Rs 15 thousand crores of investment, Noida will turn into a aerotropolis, UP officials have confirmed us to extend the Greater Noida metro upto Jewar to provide a multi modal transport facility in the airport area." 
The new airport will boost economic activity for export oriented cities like Moradabad and Aligarh in Western Uttar Pradesh, Sinha further added.?
