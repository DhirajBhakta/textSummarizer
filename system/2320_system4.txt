(Eds: Repeating after replacing word ladies with women) Mumbai, Jul 12 (PTI) In a bid to empower women, Central Railway has appointed all women staff at suburban Matunga station, a senior official said. 
A total of 30 women staffers, including 11 booking clerks, five RPF personnel, seven ticket checkers are working under the supervision of station manager Mamta Kulkarni since last two weeks, said the official. 
In my career spanning 25 years with the Railways I never thought of working with all women staff," said Kulkarni, who holds the credit of becoming first station master in Mumbai division when she joined Central Railway in 1992.
