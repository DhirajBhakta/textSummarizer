Ending a three-decade-old howitzer drought, the Indian Army is all set to receive its first heavy ordnance as the first two guns out of the 145 M777 ultra light artillery guns it ordered from the US will be arriving today, a month ahead of schedule.The two 155mm/39 caliber ultra light howitzers will be firing at the Pokhran field firing range in Rajasthan today.
