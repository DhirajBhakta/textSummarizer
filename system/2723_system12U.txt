Ahmedabad, Jul 3 (PTI) Running hookah bars in Gujarat will now draw a maximum jail term of three years with President Pranab Mukherjee giving his assent to a bill which proposes a complete ban on such joint in the state. 
"After giving his approval, the governor had sent the Bill to the president, who gave his assent recently. 
From now onwards, the state government will take strict action as per the new Act against those, who found to be running such hookah joints in the state," the minister said.
