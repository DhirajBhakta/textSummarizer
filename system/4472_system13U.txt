Multiple world champion Manny Pacquiao has agreed to fight Britains Olympic silver medallist Amir Khan on 23 April, with both boxers on Sunday confirming their intentions to appear in a super fight. 
The Australian welterweights dream fight was slated for the same date in April with Brisbane a likely host, but the school teachers hopes have now been extinguished.Negotiations between team Pacquiao and team Khan have come to terms for the April 23 bout as this is what the fans wanted, Pacquiao tweeted.
