 Property tax defaulters may be forced to consider clearing their dues with the Municipal Corporation of Gurugram (MCG), as the civic body has decided to auction 20 properties of defaulters on August 8 after failing to recover the arrears.The MCG has sealed 519 buildings in the city, all of which now face the possibility of being auctioned off.Any building or land situated within the jurisdiction of the MCG is liable to property tax.
