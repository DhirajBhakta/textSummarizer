After reports of a Rs 1000-crore Mahabharata film starring Mohanlal, a Ramayana film is reportedly in the works.According to a report in Mumbai Mirror, three film producers, Allu Aravind, Madhu Mantena and Namit Malhotra are coming together to adapt Ramayana for the big screen. 
The film will made on an estimated budget of Rs 500 crore and will be released in three languages; Hindi, Telugu and Tamil.Moreover, the new Ramayana film will be filmed in 3D and it will be released in three parts similar to the Lord of the Rings series.
