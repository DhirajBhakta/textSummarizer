Vidya plays the role of a brothels madam in the film, set against the backdrop of freedom struggle. 
"All the abuses which I cant give otherwise, I said it here," Vidya joked at the trailer launch of the movie. 
The actress said expletives are not uncommon in that area her character comes from so they stuck with that. 
Vidya said the entire team of the film worked hard to get the perfect look for her character.
