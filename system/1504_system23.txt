Canadian-born Indian YouTube sensation Lilly Singh has emerged as the Favourite YouTube Star 2017 at the 43rd People's Choice Awards! 
Lilly, who also goes by the name IISuperWomanII, was up against some stiff competition, with YouTubers PewDiePie, Shane Dawson, Tyler Oakley, and Miranda Sings, but walked away as 2017's favourite star.Singh learned of her win on the People's Choice Awards red carpet.
