Traffic to tourist destinations Shimla, Manali and Dalhousie were cut off from the rest of Himachal Pradesh on Saturday following heavy snowfall overnight, officials said.Electricity supply was snapped in Shimla and Manali as power lines were damaged due to falling trees.Nearly 50 tourists are stranded in Kothi, Manali due to road blockade. 
Efforts are being made to rescue them.Traffic bound for Shimla was disrupted at Shogi, about 15 km from Shimla, due to snowfall, an official said.Likewise, traffic on the National Highway-21 was also blocked near Patlikuhl, 20 km before Manali.
