On February 14, the day Aero India 2017 begins, the IAF will be the proud owner of its very own indigenous all-weather airborne early warning and control system popularly known as the AEW&C. 
This aerial platform is meant to be a force multiplier that will guide the IAF's fighter aircraft during combat. 
It will have the capability to detect incoming fighters, cruise missiles and even drones from both Pakistan and China.For Dr. 
Christopher, Chairman, DRDO it will be a fine day indeed. 
He was earlier the Programme Director (airborne early warning and control system) and Director, Centre for Air-Borne Systems in the DRDO before being elevated to the topmost post.
