Pramod, a 35-year-old man from Chikkamagaluru, Bengaluru who was working as a manager in one of the most popular chain of cafes, Cafe Coffee Day committed suicide. 
Pramod, who was working with the company since 11 years was promised a raise by his senior officers many time, was working undue stress and pressure. 
He blamed Cafe Coffee Day for making false promises and withholding salaries when the target wasn't met. 
Pramod left a daughter and wife behind.Here's what his suicide note said: "Cafe Coffee Day is responsible for my death. 
I was working in Bangalore. 
I returned to Bangalore as they said. 
Also, there is too much of sales pressure in the Coffee Day. 
All are working for the sake of making their ends meet, for the daily bread. 
Dear father, mother, brother, sister-in-law, father-in-law and brother-in-law, please take good care of Muddu (daughter) and Arpita (wife).
