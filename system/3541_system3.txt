Midnapore (WB), Apr 30 (PTI) A West Bengal BJP leader today described Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee as a eunuch, while accusing her of practising the politics of appeasement. 
While addressing a party meeting at Chandrakona in West Midnapore district, the partys state committee member Shyamapada Mondal alleged that Banerjee has been doing what Muslims do as their religious practice. 
"Mamata Banerjee is practising the politics of appeasement and indulging in theatrics. 
We cannot understand whether Mamata Banerjee is a man or a woman.
