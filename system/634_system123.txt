Actor Sugandha Mishra, who has part been of multiple comedy shows such as Comedy Circus Ke Taansen and Kapil Sharma show, now stars in the recently launched show, The Drama Company.  
While she agrees that the position of female comedians has improved, she says it will take time for more progress, as Indian comedy is a male-dominated field.Its more difficult for a good-looking girl to enter comedy, because such girls usually  want to do acting first and look pretty.
