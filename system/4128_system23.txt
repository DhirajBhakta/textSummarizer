Forced to drink acid by her former boyfriend in south Delhis Sangam Vihar on Wednesday morning, a 19-year-old woman was left hospitalised with a burnt throat and face.Having received around 20 per cent burns, the woman, Muskan (name changed), is undergoing treatment in the emergency ward of AIIMS. 
She tried to use signs to tell me that the acid had entered her stomach, said the mother who too received minor burns to her hands because of the acid on her daughters body.Police were informed and the woman rushed to AIIMS.
