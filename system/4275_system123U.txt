Back in 2014, the Shivaay actor was the producer of the Marathi period film Vitti Dandu directed by Ganesh Kadam.Now, according to a report in Mumbai Mirror, Ajay Devgn has teamed up with Abhinav Shukla and actor Nana Patekar to produce a Marathi film, to be directed by Satish Rajwade. 
He has directed films like Premachi Goshta, Ti Saddhya Kay Karte and Mumbai Delhi Mumbai.Nana Patekar and Ajay Devgn has co-starred in several films such as Bhoot, Apaharan and Rajneeti.
