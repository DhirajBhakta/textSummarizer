New Delhi, Jul 5 (PTI) A Parliamentary panel is understood to have sought details from the government on the Air India disinvestment decision. 
Sources said the governments decision to divest stake in Air India was discussed today at the meeting of the Department-related Parliamentary Standing Committee on Transport, Tourism and Culture. 
After the discussion, it was decided to seek an explanation on the reasons behind the disinvestment decision from the civil aviation ministry.
