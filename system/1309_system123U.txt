New Delhi, Feb 3 (PTI) The famous Mughal Gardens of Rashtrapati Bhavan will this year have special roses named after President Pranab Mukherjee and his wife Suvra Mukherjee. 
"For the first time, two new roses named after President Pranab Mukherjee and his wife late Suvra Mukherjee have been put on display. 
The special roses are developed by plant breeders from Pushpanjali rose nursery, Jakpur, West Bengal.
