Agriculture minister Pandurang Fundkar tops the list of absentees.As per the states reply to a Right to Information (RTI) application filed by activist Anil Galgali, of the 35 cabinet meetings held in the past 11 months, Fundkar has missed 11.He said, I could not attend three meetings, as I had to be present at other government and statutory functions. 
I missed a few because I was on an official foreign tour, as part of the states delegation.
