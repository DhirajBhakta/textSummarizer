From Seema Hakhu Kachru Houston, Mar 17 (PTI) An American business group will organise a conference here to discuss the latest developments and best practices of doing business in India. 
The two-day Houston India conference conference will begin from March 24. 
The theme of the Conference is Make in India  The Inside Story. 
The conference is designed to bring various constituencies that have an interest in India together and discuss with them the latest developments and the best practices of doing business in India, the organisers said.
