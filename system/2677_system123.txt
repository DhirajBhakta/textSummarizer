She wanted to fly high, travel the world by becoming an airhostess. 
But her world came crashing down on Tuesday (July 4). 
A 21-year-old woman was stabbed multiple times by a man on a busy street in Mansarovar Park area of East Delhi. 
The incident was captured by CCTV cameras installed in the neighbourhood. 
The woman was admitted to GTB Hospital where she succumbed to her injuries today.
