Jammu and Kashmir chief minister Mehbooba Mufti on Saturday accused China of meddling in the troubled state that has recently witnessed a spike in violence. 
The chief minister was speaking to reporters emerging from a meeting with Union home minister Rajnath Singh with whom she discussed the security situation in the state. 
Militants have stepped up attacks in recent months on government forces in the state as counter-insurgency operations intensify. 
She termed the attack a conspiracy to whip up communal tension in the stateEnsuring security of Amarnath Yatra obviously topped the agenda of the meeting, said an aide to the home minister. 
The home ministry in consultation with the state government has decided to erect 21 more security pickets on the Amarnath route to check whether any vehicle carrying pilgrims is moving beyond the deadline of 7pm in the Valley. 
The ill-fated bus that came under attack in the states Anantnag district had violated the deadline.
