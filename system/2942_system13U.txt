Delhi Police issued an advisory to all security agencies across the country to take appropriate security measures at places such as crowded metro stations, airports, inter-state bus terminals, railway stations, five-star hotels, embassies of western countries, busy markets and religious places. 
Hence, an advisory has been issued on June 12 to alert security agencies such as Central Industrial Security Force (CISF), other para military forces and the intelligence wing of Delhi Police to be on their toes," said a senior official of Delhi police.
