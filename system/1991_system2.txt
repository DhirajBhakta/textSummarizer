The unit is in Noida Special Economic Zone (NSEZ). 
Smuggled gold has been in high demand in India post demonetisation since it is being considered a safe commodity to launder black money that is mostly available in India in old high denomination notes. 
On examination, the consignment was found to contain mostly copper chains with zero gold content. 
Another consignment of the exporter was intercepted at NSEZ factory premises ready for export to UAE. 
In all, out of 35 kg declared gold jewellery, about 85-90% was found to be copper alloy and other low-value metal alloys. 
The actual imported high purity gold was diverted to the local market, the DRI statement said.Follow up searches are being conducted by DRI officers.
