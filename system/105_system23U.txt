Protesting against the forcible acquisition of farm land for the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC), farm activists plan to go on an indefinite fast from August 1 in Raigad district under the banner of Corridor Virodhi Shetkari Sangharsh Samiti and Jagatikaran Virodhi Kruti Samiti.The group represents 78 villages, which were originally part of the project. 
In the first phase of the project, only 10 villages are involved with the land spread over 3,100 hectares.
