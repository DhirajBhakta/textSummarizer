An unruly passenger flew all the way from Dubai to New Delhi tied to his seat as the crew aboard an Indigo flight, 6E-024, had to take the drastic step for his fellow passengers safety soon after take-off on Tuesday evening.Sources said the man turned violent  started shouting, pushed passengers and ran along the aisle  when the plane took off from Dubai.After the flight landed at 10:40 pm in New Delhi on Tuesday, he was escorted out with the help of Central Industrial Security Force (CISF) personnel, who guard the airport.
