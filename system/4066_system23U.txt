Indian television actor Parth Samthaan, who became a household name, thanks to the success of his show Kaisi Yeh Yaariyan, is in deep trouble.A complaint of molestation has been lodged against the actor by a 20-year-old model. 
Bangar Nagar police station has registered the FIR under section 354 A of the Indian Penal Code. 
The FIR was filed last month, as the incident had apparently occurred on February 20.
