Congress vice-president Rahul Gandhi today said he will be taking a break from politics to visit his grandmother abroad, triggering taunts from the opposition BJP which called the visit the "picnic" of a "kid during his summer vacations"."
He did not mention how many days will he be out and where in Italy was he headed to.Rahul's grandmother Paola Maino lives in Italy. 
In March, Rahul had visited the US, where his mother and Congress president Sonia Gandhi had gone for a health check-up. 
"When we were kids, then we used to go to the nanihaal (maternal grandmother's place) during our summer vacations," senior BJP leader from Madhya Pradesh Kailash Vijayvargiya said."
