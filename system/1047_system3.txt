If you are a Samsung Galaxy Note fan, you were waiting to hear these words -- Note 7 will be sold in India. 
Despite all the bad whether it went through before being laid to the grave, Note 7 might come back from the dead; it has to if the company is to minimize losses and environmental penalties incurred in disposal of close to 3 million devices.Update: Samsung has denied that it has any plans to sell refurbished Galaxy Note 7 units in India. 
Samsung Electronics will finalise the designs by May this year and will go on sale in June (expected). 
The battery is said to be of a lower capacity -- about 3000mAh, and if Samsung is redesigning the chassis, it might not support a dual curved screen. 
But it will be called refurbished and the price expected should be considerably cheaper.
