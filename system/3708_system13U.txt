The Supreme Court today came down hard on the government over the issue of making Aadhaar compulsory in complete violation of the apex court's previous order."
Is making Aadhaar by force the only way to tackle fraud" a furious Supreme Court told the Centre while hearing a PIL against the government's decision making Aadhaar compulsory. 
The Supreme Court bench led by Justice Sikri questioned the Centre as to how Aadhar can be made compulsory and forced on people.
