With half-a-dozen armed security guards around a truck, you would think it is carrying some precious cargo like jewellery or electronic goods. 
But it is actually carrying only tomatoes.With tomato prices going through the roof, there is a scramble to procure whatever is available and the risk of theft has increased.Ironically, many farmers in Madhya Pradesh dumped tomatoes on the roads after a bumper production brought its price to lower than Re 1 per kg about a month ago.
