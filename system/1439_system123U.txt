With less than two days' fuel left in pollbound Manipur, the Centre deployed the mighty C-17 Globemaster, the Indian Air Force's largest military transport aircraft, to airlift 96,000 litres of fuel. 
This happened after Indian Oil Corporation made a formal "urgent" request to the IAF, requesting an airlift, from Guwahati's Borjhar Air Force station to Imphal.Official sources confirmed that since Sunday, three sorties have been made.
