In the Uttar Pradesh Assembly Elections of 2017, the Samajwadi Party might have lost severely to the Bharatiya Janata Party but Rampur MLA and outgoing Urban Development Minister Azam Khan managed to win his constituency for the nineth time, that too, with a decent margin of votes.
