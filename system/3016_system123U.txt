Lucknow, Jun 23 (PTI) The Uttar Pradesh government today decided to recruit one thousand contractual doctors and also finalised the transfer policy for doctors already employed in state hospitals. 
The decision was taken at a cabinet meeting chaired by Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath. 
"The cabinet has decided to employ 1,000 doctors including 500 specialists on a contract of one year. 
The contract might be extended for two more years depending on their performance", state Health Minister Sidharth Nath Singh told reporters here.
