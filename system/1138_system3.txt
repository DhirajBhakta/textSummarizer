The BJP on Wednesday released a poster from Raigad district where the Shivsena and Congress forged a local front alleging tie-up between the two parties.The BJP has alleged that what was initially a tactict understanding has now come out in the open. 
The poster which has raised many eyebrows is from the Vadkhal-vashi segment of the Raigad Zilla Parishad elections. 
Speaking to the reporters about the poster, BJP Spokesperson Madhav Bhandari said, "We have always been talking about an understanding between the Congress and Shivsena. 
The Party soughted a report from the local leadership about the posters.
