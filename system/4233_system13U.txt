The United States today said it is working with India and Nuclear Supplies Group (NSG) members to push for New Delhi's membership in the elite grouping, indicating that there is no change in America's policy on the issue under the Trump administration."
The United States supports India's full membership in the four multilateral export control regimes, and we believe that India is ready for NSG membership," a State Department spokesperson told PTI.The spokesperson was responding to questions on the position of the Trump administration on India's bid to be a member of the 48-member NSG.The US and India have been working on this issue since the Bush Administration.
