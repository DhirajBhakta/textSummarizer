Now, Raj Thackeray-led Maharashtra Navnirman Sena (MNS) has written letters to building constructors and asked them to stop discrimination while selling flats in their buildings. 
In the letter, MNS requests various realty developers of Mumbai not to discriminate between people on the basis of caste, creed and religion.One such letter was written to Shri Chamunda Realtors by MNS spokesperson Sandeep Deshpande.
