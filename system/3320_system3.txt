It is going to be MODI (Maker Of Developed India) festival for 21 days when the Narendra Modi led BJP government will complete three years in power on May 26. 
Just like the last year's mega show at India Gate, it will be an equally grand affair this year minus the glitz.The Modi government has decided to celebrate its completion of three years in power from May 26 to June 15.
