The boardroom battle between Tata Sons and its former chairman refuses to end with Cyrus Mistry being removed as the director of Tata Industries.The announcement was made by a spokesperson of Tata Sons today. 
According to sources in the company, Mistry was voted out at the EGM (Extraordinary General Meeting) of the Tata Industries.Along with losing the position of the company director, Mistry also ceases to be its chairman.
