Six employees of a factory that manufactured electronic goods were burnt to death after a fire engulfed a five-storey building in Noidas Sector 11 on Wednesday.The incident was reported around 2.30pm at the factory that is located in F Block of Sector 11 near the Delhi-Noida border, police said. 
Prima facie it appears that a short circuit may have triggered the fire, said Aman Sharma, fire officer.Police said that the death toll may rise as there might be other bodies in the basement of the building.
