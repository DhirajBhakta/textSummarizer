 The Bhandup police arrested a 27-year-old man on Saturday for stabbing his neighbour. 
Police said the man, Akash Pande, and the 26-year-old woman were living at Tulshetpada in Bhandup. 
As a result, the relationship between the two families became strained.On June 27, Pande barged inside the womans home and stabbed her on the stomach in front of her family, police said.
