The Delhi High Court on Thursday stayed the Meghalaya State Commission for Womens (MSCW) order summoning the Delhi Golf Club secretary over the incident involving a Khasi woman being asked to leave the premier club allegedly because of her attire.Justice Vibhu Bakhru also sought response from Meghalaya womens commission by September 8, the next date of hearing, on a petition filed by the Delhi Golf Club challenging the commissions July 3 order.
