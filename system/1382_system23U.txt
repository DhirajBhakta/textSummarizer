A gang that extorted money from politicians, ministers and bureaucrats by impersonating as top ranked political leaders of the Congress and the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) was busted by the Delhi police on Friday. 
Ravindra Yadav, joint commissioner of police (crime) said that posing as Ram Madhav, BJPs national general secretary, Tiwari allegedly tried to extort 10 lakh from Amar Kumar Bauri, minister of revenue, registration and land in the Jharkhand government.
