A week after three unidentified bikers robbed a cash van outside an ATM outlet in east Delhis Pandav Nagar, the Delhi Police on Monday claimed to have solved the daylight robbery case, the first such crime after demonetisation.The three men have been arrested and Rs 9 lakh were recovered from them. 
The cash van staff initially told police that the robbed amount was Rs 5 lakh.In the FIR, the police mentioned robbery of Rs 5 lakh only.
