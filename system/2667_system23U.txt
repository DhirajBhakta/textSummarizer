Chandigarh, Jul 6 (PTI) The Centre has approved renaming of Ganda and Kinnar villages in Fatehabad and Hisar districts of Haryana. 
Union Ministry of Home Affairs has approved the renaming of two villages in Haryana, an official spokesman said here this evening. 
Village Ganda has been renamed as Ajit Nagar, whereas village Kinnar has been rechristened as Gaibi Nagar, the official said.
