India captain Virat Kohli on Monday hosted a charity ball in London for the Justice and Care organisation. 
The charity event was attended by former India captain Sachin Tendulkar and Kohli's teammates Yuvraj Singh, Mahendra Singh Dhoni, Rohit Sharma, Shikhar Dhawan along with other members of the Indian team and it was hosted by renowned cricket presenter Alan Wilkins.Dhawan was accompanied to the event by his wife, Ayesha Mukherjee and son Zoravar, while Rohit's wife Ritika Sajdeh was also in attendance.Off to @virat.kohli charity dinner with the Zoravar gang !
