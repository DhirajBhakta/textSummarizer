A British actor who worked on EastEnders and Channel 4 drama Ackley Bridge has been rescued three days after falling from the top of a jungle waterfall and smashing his leg on the Thai island of Koh Samui.Paul Nicholls, 38, was found in agony at the base of Khun Si falls after a local spotted his abandoned rental motorbike, which police traced to his real name  Gerrard Paul Greenhalgh  prompting a search.He was found partially submerged in a rock pool, semi-conscious and with hypothermia, unable to move to safety or find food.I am lucky to be alive, Nicholls said, according to the Sun.
