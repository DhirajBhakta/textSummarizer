Bollywood actor Shahid Kapoor says his upcoming film Padmavati, marred by controversies, is made with a good intent.Shahid, who won the Big Zee Entertainment Award for the best actor  for Udta Punjab on Saturday, spoke to the media on the sidelines of the event.Talking about the controversies surrounding the historical drama, whose shooting was disrupted for showing a romantic angle between Rajput queen Padmavati and Alauddin Khilji, Shahid said: ?
According to me, I have not shot any scene which has been changed. 
I think there were a lot of presumptions about the film, and people were presuming about the scenes.?
I hope when people see this film, they will realize that the heart of the film and intent of the film is very good,?
