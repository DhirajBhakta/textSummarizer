Former Bihar governor Ram Nath Kovind took oath as the 14th President of India today at the historic Central Hall in Parliament.With this, Ram Nath Kovind, who was born in Paraukh village of Kanpur, is also the first Uttar Pradesh-born President of India. 
He served as President from January 26, 1950 to 14 May, 1962.Neelam Sanjiva ReddyThe seventh presidential election was held in 1977 and Neelam Sanjiva Reddy was elected unopposed to the post, the only one to have achieved this feat till date. 
Reddy was 63 years old when he took over as the President, making him the youngest to hold the office till date. 
He was the only bachelor to have held the country's highest office.Pratibha PatilIn 2007, Pratibha Patil became the first woman to occupy the post of President.
