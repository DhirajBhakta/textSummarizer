New Delhi, Jun 22 (PTI) If Air India is to be privatised, then Tatas will be one of the better candidates to buy stake in the airline, according to business leader Sunil Mittal. 
"Tatas, if it (Air India) has to be privatised, should be one of the better candidates," Mittal said here. 
His response came when reporters sought his views on reports that Air India might be sold off and that Tatas are interested in buying stake in the airline. 
A senior Civil Aviation Ministry official today said the Cabinet would first decide on the future course of action for Air India. 
On Wednesday, sources said Tata group was evaluating options on buying stake in the airline.
