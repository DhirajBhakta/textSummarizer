The Goa Suraksha Manch (GSM), which is backed by state Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) chief Subhash Velingkar, has used a similar sing-song routine to question chief minister Manohar Parrikar over his purported support to the casino lobby and failure of governance.The video Mannu Aamka Tuzyawar Bharosa Nai Hai, which has gone viral, features workers of the organisation questioning Mannu (Manohar Parrikar) backing the casino industry.Mannu we do not trust you, Mannu is your humanity no more, You are a protector of casinos, You are a destroyer of Goa, says the video.
