MS DHONI'S AADHAR REGISTRATION DETAILS LEAKEDThis revelation comes just days after CSC e-governance services on Tuesday tweeted a photo in which Dhoni was seen authenticating fingerprints to avail Aadhaar services.However, along with the photo of Dhoni, CSC's official Twitter account also tweeted his Aadhaar registration details in a screenshot, which then prompted his wife Sakshi to post a series of furious tweets.Sakshi tagged Law minister Ravi Shankar Prasad in her tweets, forcing him to take prompt action.The Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) - the organization that handles the Aadhar project - has blacklisted the agency that leaked the Aadhaar receipt Dhoni for 10 years.
