The first look of Half Girlfriend is everything romantic. 
Arjun Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor are set to get drenched in love with their upcoming film. 
Arjun Kapoor and Shraddha Kapoor. pic.twitter.com/1EQWmC4GhG The film is based on Chetan Bhagat's novel of the same name, and revolves around the story of a Bihari boy who falls in love with a rich Delhi girl. 
In an earlier interview to IANS, Arjun had said that Half Girlfriend is not any frivolous romantic comedy. 
It's a romantic journey and for me that is very exciting," he said.Directed by Mohit Suri, the film is set to hit the screens on May 19.
