Beijing, Dec 20 (PTI) With 40,917 film screens, China has overtaken the US for the first time as the total number of screens in America stood at 40,759. 
There are 40,917 film screens in Chinese mainland cinemas as of December 20, according to the film bureau of the State Administration of Press, Publication, Radio, Film and Television (SARFT) today. 
The film industry has seen huge growth, with 26 film screens on average added each day during 2016, it said, adding that 85 per cent of all new screens can play 3D movies.
