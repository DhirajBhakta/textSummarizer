The Navys new destroyer, a domestically designed and produced vessel, was launched at the Jiangnan Shipyard (Group), Shanghai. 
The vessel is the first of Chinas new generation of destroyers. 
The vessel marks a milestone in improving the nations Navy armament system and building a strong and modern Navy. 
The naval formation includes destroyers Jinan and Yinchuan, frigate Yantai and a squadron of J-15 fighter jets and helicopters, a Chinese defence ministry statement said.
