In a freak incident, which delayed the Air India flight by an hour, a catering van hit the door of an ATR aircraft at Delhi?
Air India officials are assessing the damage and said that the loss will run into lakhs and the aircraft will also remain grounded till it is repaired.?
The catering vehicle was returning after loading the food in the aircraft. 
said an airport source.About 70 passengers, who were to fly to Gorakhpur by the aircraft, and were ready to board, had to wait for another hour. 
The flight, which was scheduled to take off at 9:45 am, took off at 10:45 am. 
The passengers were shifted to a different aircraft.?
The vehicle hit the service gate and it is completely damaged now.
