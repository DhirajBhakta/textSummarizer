The massive push given by the central government to its ambitious Digital India program has led to a massive jump in internet users in the country.Minister of Communications Manoj Sinha, in a written reply to the Rajya Sabha, said that as per information received from Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI), there were 391.50 million Internet subscribers as on December 31, 2016.Referring to Telecom companies' body National Association of Software and Services Companies (NASSCOM) and Akamai, the Telecom Ministry said that 730 million Internet users are anticipated in the country by 2020."
To give a push to digitisation in country, the government has allocated 965 Megahertz spectrum through auction in October, 2016 to various telecom service providers for access services.
